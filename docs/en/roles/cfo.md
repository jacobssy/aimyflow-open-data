## Core Skills

- **Financial Planning**: Builds budgets, forecasts, and long-range plans to guide company performance.
- **Cash Flow Management**: Monitors liquidity, funding needs, and capital allocation to keep operations financed.
- **Financial Reporting**: Oversees accurate financial statements, close processes, and management reporting.
- **Risk & Compliance**: Maintains controls, regulatory compliance, and financial risk oversight across the business.
- **Capital Strategy**: Evaluates financing, investment, and M&A choices to support enterprise value.
