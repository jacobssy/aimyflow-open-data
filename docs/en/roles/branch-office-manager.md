## Core Skills

- **Branch Operations Control**: Oversees daily branch activity, service flow, staffing coverage, and local issue resolution.
- **P&L Management**: Manages revenue, controllable costs, and branch targets to deliver sustainable financial performance.
- **Sales Execution**: Drives local sales plans, pipeline follow-up, and conversion activity in line with regional goals.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Ensures branch processes, documentation, cash handling, and staff practices meet internal and legal requirements.
- **Performance Monitoring**: Tracks branch KPIs, audits results, and corrective actions to improve service and target attainment.
