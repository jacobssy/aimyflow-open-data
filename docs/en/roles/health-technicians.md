## Core Skills

- **Specimen Collection**: Collects blood, tissue, or other specimens correctly for accurate testing and safe handling.
- **Diagnostic Equipment Operation**: Operates diagnostic instruments according to protocol and verifies proper function before use.
- **Test Processing**: Prepares, processes, and documents laboratory or clinical tests with consistent technical accuracy.
- **Quality Control**: Checks controls, calibration, and result consistency to maintain reliable clinical output.
- **Infection Control**: Applies sterilization, PPE, and biohazard procedures to protect patients, staff, and samples.
