## Core Skills

- **Community Mapping**: Identifies priority audiences, local networks, and access barriers to target outreach where it will matter most.
- **Partnership Outreach**: Builds and maintains relationships with schools, community groups, funders, and service partners to expand program reach.
- **Campaign Coordination**: Plans outreach events, calendars, materials, and follow-up workflows so campaigns run on time and on message.
- **Impact Tracking**: Tracks participation, response rates, referrals, and conversion patterns to evaluate outreach performance and improve future efforts.
- **Ethical Messaging**: Shapes public-facing messages that are accurate, trauma-informed, culturally appropriate, and aligned with nonprofit policies.
