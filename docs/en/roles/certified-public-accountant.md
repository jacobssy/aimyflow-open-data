## Core Skills

- **Financial Reporting**: Prepare compliant financial statements and disclosures under applicable accounting standards.
- **Audit Execution**: Plan and perform audit procedures to evaluate evidence, controls, and material misstatements.
- **Tax Compliance**: Prepare tax returns and ensure filings, calculations, and positions comply with tax law.
- **Internal Control Review**: Assess control design and operating effectiveness to identify risks and control deficiencies.
- **Accounting Research**: Interpret complex transactions and accounting guidance to support defensible technical conclusions.
