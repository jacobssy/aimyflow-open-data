## Core Skills

- **Network Planning**: Designs warehouse and transport flows to meet service levels at controlled cost.
- **Capacity Forecasting**: Forecasts order and shipment volumes to align labor, fleet, and space requirements.
- **Routing & Scheduling**: Builds shipment schedules and routing plans that improve utilization and delivery reliability.
- **Inventory Flow Control**: Sets replenishment and transfer rules to keep inventory available without excess stock.
- **Transport Compliance**: Applies shipping, customs, and safety requirements so operations run without regulatory breaches.
