## Core Skills

- **Case Assessment**: Analyzes charges, facts, and procedural posture to identify viable defense strategies early.
- **Evidence Review**: Examines witness statements, forensic material, and records for inconsistencies, gaps, and suppression issues.
- **Motion Practice**: Drafts and argues motions on bail, suppression, dismissal, and other procedural defenses.
- **Plea Negotiation**: Negotiates charge reductions or sentencing terms based on evidentiary risk and client priorities.
- **Trial Advocacy**: Builds courtroom defense through cross-examination, objections, witness handling, and persuasive argument.
