## Core Skills

- **Operational Strategy**: Turns company goals into operating models, priorities, and execution plans across functions.
- **Process Optimization**: Improves core processes to raise efficiency, quality, scalability, and service performance.
- **Performance Management**: Defines KPIs, reviews operating results, and drives corrective actions to keep targets on track.
- **Cross-Functional Alignment**: Aligns operations with finance, sales, product, and HR to remove bottlenecks and support growth.
- **Operational Risk & Compliance**: Builds controls, governance, and contingency plans to manage operational risk and regulatory obligations.
