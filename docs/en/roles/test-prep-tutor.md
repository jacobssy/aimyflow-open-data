## Core Skills

- **Diagnostic Assessment**: Identifies baseline ability, score gaps, and target exam requirements to shape instruction.
- **Study Plan Design**: Builds a realistic preparation schedule with milestones, practice load, and review cycles.
- **Exam Strategy Coaching**: Teaches timing, question selection, elimination methods, and score-maximizing test tactics.
- **Targeted Skill Remediation**: Addresses recurring errors in tested content areas through focused drills and clear explanations.
- **Progress Tracking**: Uses mock results and homework data to adjust pacing, priorities, and score goals.
