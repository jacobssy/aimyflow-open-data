## Core Skills

- **Press Setup**: Sets plates, paper, ink, and registration to prepare the press for production.
- **Color Control**: Monitors ink density and color consistency to meet proof and job standards.
- **Print Quality Inspection**: Checks sheets for defects such as misregistration, smudging, streaks, or uneven coverage.
- **Production Run Control**: Keeps the press running at target speed while maintaining output, waste, and job accuracy.
- **Press Maintenance**: Cleans, lubricates, and inspects press components to prevent faults and keep print quality stable.
