## Core Skills

- **Technical Design**: Translate requirements into feasible system designs, specifications, and engineering drawings.
- **Analysis & Simulation**: Use calculations, models, and simulations to predict performance and verify design choices.
- **Testing & Validation**: Plan and run tests to confirm products meet functional, safety, and reliability targets.
- **Standards Compliance**: Apply codes, standards, and regulatory requirements throughout design, build, and release.
- **Root Cause Engineering**: Investigate failures in the field or production and implement corrective engineering changes.
