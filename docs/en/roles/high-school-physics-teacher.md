## Core Skills

- **Curriculum Mapping**: Aligns physics units, pacing, and learning goals with grade-level standards and exam requirements.
- **Laboratory Instruction**: Designs and supervises experiments that build measurement technique, data quality, and physical reasoning.
- **Quantitative Problem Solving**: Teaches students to model situations, apply formulas correctly, and justify multi-step solutions.
- **Assessment Design**: Builds quizzes, lab reports, and exams that accurately measure conceptual understanding and calculation skill.
- **Lab Safety Compliance**: Maintains safe lab procedures, equipment handling rules, and risk controls for school physics activities.
