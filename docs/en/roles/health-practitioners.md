## Core Skills

- **Clinical Assessment**: Evaluates symptoms, history, and exam findings to determine likely conditions and care urgency.
- **Diagnostic Testing**: Selects appropriate tests and interprets results in the context of the patient’s presentation.
- **Treatment Planning**: Builds treatment plans that balance diagnosis, risk, evidence, and patient-specific needs.
- **Medication Management**: Prescribes, adjusts, and monitors medications for effectiveness, interactions, and safety.
- **Clinical Documentation**: Maintains accurate records that support continuity of care, legal compliance, and billing.
