## Core Skills

- **Lesson Sequencing**: Plans math lessons that build concepts step by step from concrete models to abstract reasoning.
- **Numeracy Instruction**: Teaches number sense, operations, and mental strategies using age-appropriate representations and practice.
- **Formative Assessment**: Checks student understanding during instruction and adjusts tasks, pacing, and support based on evidence.
- **Intervention Planning**: Designs targeted support for students who need reinforcement, extension, or accommodations in math learning.
- **Manipulative-Based Teaching**: Uses manipulatives, drawings, and visual models to make mathematical relationships visible and understandable.
