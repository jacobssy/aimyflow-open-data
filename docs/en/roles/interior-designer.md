## Core Skills

- **Space Planning**: Develops efficient room layouts that support circulation, function, and client needs.
- **Material Specification**: Selects finishes, surfaces, and furnishings based on aesthetics, durability, and maintenance.
- **Technical Drafting**: Produces detailed drawings and specifications for contractors, fabricators, and permit review.
- **Code Compliance**: Integrates building, fire, accessibility, and safety requirements into the interior design.
- **Project Coordination**: Coordinates vendors, consultants, budgets, and installation schedules to deliver the design.
