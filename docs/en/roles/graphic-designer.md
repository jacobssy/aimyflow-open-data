## Core Skills

- **Visual Identity Design**: Builds coherent brand assets such as logos, color systems, and typography for consistent use.
- **Layout Composition**: Arranges text, images, and spacing to guide attention and improve readability across formats.
- **Production Artwork**: Prepares press-ready or digital files with correct dimensions, bleed, resolution, and exports.
- **Typography Application**: Selects and applies typefaces, hierarchy, and spacing that match the message and medium.
- **Design Specification Compliance**: Applies brand guidelines, asset standards, and delivery specs accurately across design outputs.
