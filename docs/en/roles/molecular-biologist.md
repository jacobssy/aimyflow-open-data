## Core Skills

- **Experimental Design**: Designs molecular experiments with appropriate controls, sample strategy, and measurable endpoints.
- **Nucleic Acid Techniques**: Executes DNA and RNA extraction, PCR, cloning, and related workflows with reliable laboratory practice.
- **Gene Expression Analysis**: Measures and interprets gene expression data using qPCR, sequencing, or hybridization-based methods.
- **Sequence Data Interpretation**: Evaluates sequence quality, identifies variants or constructs, and links results to biological questions.
- **Laboratory Documentation**: Maintains traceable records, reagent histories, and protocol updates to support reproducibility and compliance.
