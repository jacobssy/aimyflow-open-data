## Core Skills

- **Pipeline Forecasting**: Projects deal flow, conversion rates, and revenue timing to guide targets and resource decisions.
- **Territory Planning**: Designs account coverage, market segmentation, and sales capacity by territory or vertical.
- **Deal Review**: Evaluates pipeline quality, deal risks, pricing posture, and next-step discipline in active opportunities.
- **Sales Process Governance**: Standardizes stage criteria, CRM hygiene, approval flows, and inspection routines across the team.
- **Quota Management**: Sets quotas, monitors attainment trends, and adjusts plans when coverage or productivity shifts.
