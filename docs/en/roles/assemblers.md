## Core Skills

- **Blueprint Reading**: Interprets drawings, dimensions, and part lists to assemble products exactly to specification.
- **Component Assembly**: Fits, fastens, and aligns parts using hand tools, fixtures, and standard assembly methods.
- **Tolerance Control**: Checks fit, clearances, and measurements to ensure assembled parts meet required tolerances.
- **Quality Inspection**: Identifies defects, incomplete work, and damage before units move to the next production stage.
- **Work Instruction Compliance**: Follows assembly sequences, torque settings, safety rules, and documentation requirements on the line.
