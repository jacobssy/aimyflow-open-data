## Core Skills

- **Production Scheduling**: Builds and adjusts daily production schedules to meet output targets, labor capacity, and material availability.
- **Line Performance Control**: Monitors throughput, downtime, and bottlenecks on the shop floor and takes action to keep lines running efficiently.
- **Quality Deviation Handling**: Leads containment, root cause follow-up, and corrective actions when products or processes fall outside quality standards.
- **Safety and Compliance Oversight**: Enforces plant safety rules, operating procedures, and audit requirements to maintain compliant daily operations.
- **Resource and Shift Coordination**: Allocates operators, equipment, and shift coverage to match production priorities and respond to operational disruptions.
