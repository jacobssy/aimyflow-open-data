## Core Skills

- **Calendar Coordination**: Manages calendars, resolves scheduling conflicts, and keeps meetings aligned with priorities.
- **Document Preparation**: Prepares, formats, and updates correspondence, reports, and internal records accurately.
- **Travel & Meeting Logistics**: Arranges travel, meeting rooms, materials, and itineraries so activities run smoothly.
- **Records & Filing Control**: Maintains filing systems and retrieves records quickly while following retention rules.
- **Expense & Purchase Support**: Tracks expenses, processes purchase requests, and checks submissions against policy.
