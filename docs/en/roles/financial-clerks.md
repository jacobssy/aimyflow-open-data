## Core Skills

- **Transaction Processing**: Records payments, transfers, and account activity accurately within financial systems.
- **Account Reconciliation**: Matches internal records to statements and resolves routine discrepancies promptly.
- **Financial Documentation**: Prepares, checks, and files forms, vouchers, and supporting records for audit-ready documentation.
- **Regulatory Compliance Checks**: Verifies transactions and customer files against internal controls and regulatory requirements.
- **Cash and Payment Administration**: Handles receivables, disbursements, and daily cash records while maintaining accurate balances.
