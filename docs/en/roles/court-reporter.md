## Core Skills

- **Realtime Stenography**: Capture courtroom speech verbatim at high speed while maintaining speaker attribution.
- **Transcript Production**: Prepare certified transcripts with consistent formatting, punctuation, and procedural notation.
- **Record Accuracy Control**: Review the record against notes and audio to resolve ambiguities, overlaps, and inaudible passages.
- **Judicial Terminology**: Apply legal terminology, case references, and courtroom conventions accurately throughout the record.
- **Record Custody**: Maintain secure handling, certification, and timely release of official transcripts and exhibits.
