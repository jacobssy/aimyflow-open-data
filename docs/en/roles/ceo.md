## Core Skills

- **Strategic Direction**: Sets company direction, priorities, and long-term positioning based on market and business realities.
- **Capital Allocation**: Allocates capital across growth, operations, and risk with clear return and liquidity trade-offs.
- **Executive Operating Cadence**: Runs the management rhythm through goals, reviews, and corrective actions across the business.
- **Board and Governance**: Prepares board decisions, governance matters, and disclosures with sound judgment and accountability.
- **Investor and Stakeholder Management**: Maintains credibility with investors, regulators, and key partners through consistent narrative and performance.
