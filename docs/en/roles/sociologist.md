## Core Skills

- **Research Design**: Builds sociological studies with clear questions, samples, methods, and analytical logic.
- **Survey & Interview Methods**: Designs instruments and conducts surveys or interviews that capture reliable social data.
- **Qualitative Analysis**: Interprets narratives, observations, and documents to identify social patterns and meanings.
- **Statistical Analysis**: Uses statistical techniques to test relationships, compare groups, and evaluate social trends.
- **Research Ethics**: Protects participants through consent, confidentiality, and careful handling of sensitive social data.
