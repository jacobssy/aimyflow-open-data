## Core Skills

- **Office Operations Control**: Oversees daily administrative workflows, service levels, and issue resolution across office functions.
- **Budget and Cost Administration**: Plans administrative budgets, tracks spending, and controls operating costs against approved targets.
- **Policy and Compliance Oversight**: Maintains administrative policies, documentation standards, and compliance with internal and regulatory requirements.
- **Vendor and Facilities Administration**: Coordinates vendors, contracts, maintenance, and workplace services to keep facilities running reliably.
- **Records and Process Governance**: Controls document retention, approval flows, and administrative process standards for accurate, auditable operations.
