## Core Skills

- **Structural Analysis**: Calculates loads, stresses, and stability to verify safe and efficient structural designs.
- **Site and Geotechnical Assessment**: Interprets soil, groundwater, and site data to define foundations and earthwork constraints.
- **Construction Documentation**: Produces drawings, specifications, and quantity information that guide accurate construction and procurement.
- **Code and Permit Compliance**: Applies design codes, safety standards, and permit requirements throughout project development.
- **Construction Oversight**: Checks site execution, materials, and progress to ensure the work matches design intent and quality standards.
