## Core Skills

- **Patent Prosecution**: Drafts, files, and prosecutes patent applications to secure enforceable protection.
- **Trademark Portfolio Management**: Manages trademark clearance, filing, renewal, and enforcement across relevant markets.
- **IP Litigation Strategy**: Builds infringement and defense strategies using evidence, remedies, and procedural options.
- **Licensing and IP Agreements**: Negotiates and drafts licensing, assignment, and coexistence agreements that allocate IP rights clearly.
- **Freedom-to-Operate Analysis**: Assesses third-party rights and infringement risk before product launch or market entry.
