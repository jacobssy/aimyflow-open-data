## Core Skills

- **Service Floor Coordination**: Coordinates seating, service timing, and station coverage to keep meal periods running smoothly.
- **Food Safety Oversight**: Monitors hygiene, holding temperatures, and handling practices to meet food safety requirements.
- **Shift Staffing**: Builds shift plans around demand, skill mix, and break coverage to maintain service levels.
- **Inventory Control**: Tracks stock, requisitions, and waste to keep service supplied without excess loss.
- **Guest Issue Resolution**: Resolves service complaints and special requests with decisions that protect guest experience and policy.
