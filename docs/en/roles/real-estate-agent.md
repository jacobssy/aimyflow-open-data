## Core Skills

- **Property Valuation**: Estimate market value from comparable sales, location, condition, and current demand.
- **Listing Strategy**: Prepare pricing, positioning, listing content, and launch timing to attract qualified buyers.
- **Buyer Qualification**: Assess budget, financing readiness, location needs, and purchase timeline before showings.
- **Offer Negotiation**: Negotiate price, contingencies, repairs, and closing terms to secure workable agreements.
- **Transaction Compliance**: Manage disclosures, contracts, deadlines, and required documents through closing.
