## Core Skills

- **Portfolio Reporting**: Consolidates project data into portfolio reports with status, risks, dependencies, and executive metrics.
- **Governance Control**: Maintains stage gates, approval workflows, and PMO standards so projects follow defined governance rules.
- **Schedule Coordination**: Aligns milestones, cross-project dependencies, and delivery timelines to keep plans realistic and synchronized.
- **Risk & Issue Tracking**: Tracks risks, issues, and actions across projects and escalates items that threaten scope, cost, or schedule.
- **Resource Planning**: Monitors capacity, demand, and allocation across projects to identify bottlenecks and support prioritization decisions.
