## Core Skills

- **Information Architecture**: Structures complex product knowledge into clear, searchable documentation sets and navigation paths.
- **API Documentation**: Produces accurate endpoint, schema, authentication, and example content from technical specifications and tests.
- **Style Guide Management**: Defines and applies terminology, tone, syntax, and formatting standards across documentation deliverables.
- **SME Interviewing**: Elicits missing details from engineers and product specialists to turn implicit knowledge into usable guidance.
- **Documentation QA**: Verifies technical accuracy, completeness, version alignment, and task usability before publication.
