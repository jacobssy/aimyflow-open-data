## Core Skills

- **Gear Handling**: Deploys, retrieves, and maintains nets, lines, traps, and winches safely during fishing operations.
- **Catch Assessment**: Identifies target species, checks size and quality, and sorts catch to meet market and legal standards.
- **Vessel Deck Operations**: Manages deck tasks, load handling, icing, and stowage to keep the vessel efficient and stable at sea.
- **Catch Preservation**: Bleeds, cleans, chills, and stores fish correctly to preserve freshness and reduce spoilage.
- **Fishery Compliance**: Follows quotas, seasons, protected-species rules, and catch records required by fishery regulations.
