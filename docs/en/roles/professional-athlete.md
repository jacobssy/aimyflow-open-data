## Core Skills

- **Sport-Specific Technique**: Executes precise sport-specific movements with consistency under training and competition conditions.
- **Physical Conditioning**: Builds and maintains strength, endurance, speed, and mobility for peak performance demands.
- **Performance Analysis**: Reviews metrics, video, and competitive results to refine execution and correct weaknesses.
- **Competition Strategy**: Adapts pacing, positioning, and tactical choices to opponents, conditions, and match situations.
- **Recovery and Compliance**: Follows recovery protocols, nutrition plans, and anti-doping rules to sustain availability and eligibility.
