## Core Skills

- **Residential Space Planning**: Organizes rooms, circulation, storage, and daylight to support daily living patterns on constrained sites.
- **Code & Permit Documentation**: Prepares compliant drawings and submissions that satisfy residential building codes, zoning rules, and permit reviews.
- **Construction Detailing**: Develops buildable wall, roof, window, and waterproofing details that resolve performance and finish conditions.
- **Material & Finish Specification**: Selects materials and finishes for durability, maintenance, budget, interior character, and residential performance.
- **Site & Construction Coordination**: Reviews site progress, answers RFIs, and aligns contractors with drawings, specifications, and design intent during construction.
