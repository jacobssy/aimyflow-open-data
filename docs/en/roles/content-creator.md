## Core Skills

- **Content Strategy**: Plans formats, themes, and publishing cadence around audience demand and platform goals.
- **Scriptwriting**: Writes clear, platform-appropriate scripts that structure hooks, pacing, and calls to action.
- **Visual Production**: Produces photo, video, or graphic assets with consistent framing, lighting, and brand style.
- **Editing Workflow**: Edits raw material into polished content using pacing, captions, sound, and platform-specific cuts.
- **Performance Analysis**: Reviews reach, retention, and conversion metrics to refine future content decisions.
