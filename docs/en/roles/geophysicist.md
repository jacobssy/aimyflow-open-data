## Core Skills

- **Seismic Interpretation**: Interpret seismic sections and attributes to map subsurface structures, faults, and stratigraphic features.
- **Survey Design**: Design acquisition programs with suitable source, receiver, spacing, and geometry for target depth and resolution.
- **Potential Field Analysis**: Analyze gravity and magnetic data to infer basin geometry, basement depth, and large-scale structural trends.
- **Petrophysical Integration**: Integrate well logs, core data, and rock properties to constrain interpretations and reduce subsurface uncertainty.
- **Geohazard Assessment**: Assess faults, overpressure, shallow gas, and unstable ground conditions that affect drilling and site development.
