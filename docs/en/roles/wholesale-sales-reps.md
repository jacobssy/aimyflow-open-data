## Core Skills

- **Account Prospecting**: Identifies and qualifies retail, distributor, and reseller accounts that fit the product line and sales territory.
- **Price & Margin Negotiation**: Negotiates pricing, discounts, volume breaks, and payment terms while protecting margin targets.
- **Order Program Management**: Builds order plans, confirms assortments, and manages replenishment cycles for recurring wholesale business.
- **Channel Product Positioning**: Matches products, pack sizes, and promotions to each channel’s demand pattern and buyer expectations.
- **Trade Compliance Documentation**: Prepares quotes, contracts, and sales documents in line with customer requirements, policies, and trade terms.
