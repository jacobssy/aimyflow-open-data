## Core Skills

- **Leadership Assessment**: Assesses leadership behavior and development needs using interviews, feedback tools, and workplace evidence.
- **Executive Coaching Plans**: Designs structured coaching plans with clear goals, session milestones, and measurable behavior changes.
- **360 Feedback Facilitation**: Runs 360 feedback processes and turns multi-source input into actionable coaching priorities.
- **Behavior Change Tracking**: Monitors progress through observed behaviors, stakeholder check-ins, and review against coaching objectives.
- **Coaching Ethics & Boundaries**: Maintains confidentiality, manages sponsor relationships, and keeps coaching within ethical and professional boundaries.
