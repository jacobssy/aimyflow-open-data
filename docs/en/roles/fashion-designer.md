## Core Skills

- **Trend Research**: Analyzes cultural, market, and consumer signals to define commercially relevant design directions.
- **Collection Development**: Builds coherent seasonal assortments with balanced silhouettes, categories, colors, and price points.
- **Technical Sketching**: Translates design ideas into accurate flats and specifications for patternmaking and sampling.
- **Fabric & Trim Selection**: Selects fabrics, trims, and finishes that achieve the intended drape, quality, cost, and production fit.
- **Fit Review**: Evaluates prototypes on body and form to refine proportion, comfort, construction, and finish.
