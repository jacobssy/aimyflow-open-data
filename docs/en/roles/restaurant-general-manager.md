## Core Skills

- **P&L Management**: Controls revenue, labor, and operating costs to deliver target restaurant profit.
- **Service Operations**: Plans floor coverage, pacing, and shift execution to keep service smooth during peak periods.
- **Food Safety Compliance**: Enforces sanitation, storage, and audit standards to reduce food safety and regulatory risk.
- **Inventory Control**: Manages ordering, stock levels, and waste to protect availability, freshness, and margin.
- **Guest Recovery**: Resolves service failures quickly and fairly to restore guest satisfaction and protect reputation.
