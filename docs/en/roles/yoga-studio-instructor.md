## Core Skills

- **Pose Alignment**: Guides safe, precise posture alignment and gives hands-on or verbal corrections during practice.
- **Class Sequencing**: Builds balanced class flows with suitable warm-up, peak poses, transitions, and cooldown.
- **Student Adaptation**: Adapts poses and intensity for different levels, limitations, injuries, and prenatal needs.
- **Breath Cueing**: Coordinates breath cues with movement to support rhythm, focus, and safe effort.
- **Studio Safety**: Maintains a safe studio environment through screening, prop setup, spacing, and risk awareness.
