## Core Skills

- **Preventive Maintenance**: Perform scheduled inspections and servicing to keep equipment reliable and reduce unplanned failures.
- **Fault Diagnosis**: Trace mechanical, electrical, or control issues and identify the root cause before repair.
- **Repair Execution**: Replace parts, restore functions, and verify equipment returns safely to service.
- **Safety Isolation**: Apply lockout, isolation, and permit procedures before working on energized or moving equipment.
- **Maintenance Documentation**: Record work performed, parts used, and equipment status to support follow-up maintenance planning.
