## Core Skills

- **Therapy Activity Support**: Guides patients through prescribed occupational therapy exercises and daily living tasks under supervision.
- **Patient Progress Observation**: Monitors patient performance, tolerance, and functional changes during sessions and reports findings accurately.
- **Adaptive Equipment Handling**: Prepares, fits, and instructs patients in the safe use of splints, adaptive devices, and therapy tools.
- **Treatment Documentation**: Completes session notes, activity logs, and treatment records in line with clinical and legal standards.
- **Safety and Infection Control**: Maintains a safe therapy environment by following transfer precautions, sanitation procedures, and risk protocols.
