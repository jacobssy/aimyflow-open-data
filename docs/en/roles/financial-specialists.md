## Core Skills

- **Financial Analysis**: Evaluates financial statements, cash flow, and performance trends to support business decisions.
- **Budget Planning**: Builds budgets and forecasts while tracking variances against financial targets.
- **Risk Assessment**: Assesses credit, market, or operational risks and recommends appropriate controls.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Applies financial regulations, internal policies, and reporting standards in daily operations.
- **Investment Evaluation**: Reviews investment options using return, valuation, and risk metrics to guide recommendations.
