## Core Skills

- **Onboarding Strategy**: Plans onboarding milestones, adoption goals, and stakeholder actions to achieve a strong customer launch.
- **Adoption Analysis**: Interprets usage data, feature uptake, and account signals to identify adoption gaps and next steps.
- **Renewal Planning**: Builds renewal plans by tracking value realization, commercial risks, and timing across the account.
- **Escalation Management**: Coordinates urgent product, service, and support issues to protect customer outcomes and trust.
- **Success Planning**: Defines measurable customer outcomes, review cadence, and action plans aligned to business objectives.
