## Core Skills

- **Child Safety Assessment**: Assesss immediate and ongoing risk to determine whether a child can remain safely at home.
- **Case Planning**: Builds case plans with clear goals, services, timelines, and review points for child and family outcomes.
- **Protective Intervention**: Coordinates safety actions such as emergency placement, supervision, and referrals when harm or neglect is identified.
- **Court and Documentation**: Produces timely case records, affidavits, and court reports that meet legal and agency standards.
- **Family Visitation Monitoring**: Monitors parent-child visits to evaluate attachment, safety, parenting behavior, and reunification progress.
