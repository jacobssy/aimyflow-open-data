## Core Skills

- **Loom Operation**: Operates looms or knitting machines while controlling speed, tension, and fabric formation.
- **Yarn Preparation**: Prepares yarn packages, threading, and warping setups to keep production running correctly.
- **Fabric Inspection**: Checks fabric for defects, measurements, and consistency against quality standards.
- **Machine Setup**: Sets machine parameters and makes routine adjustments for style changes and stable output.
- **Textile Safety Compliance**: Follows safety, handling, and cleanliness procedures around moving equipment, fibers, and chemicals.
