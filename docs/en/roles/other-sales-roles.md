## Core Skills

- **Pipeline Qualification**: Assesses lead fit, urgency, budget, and buying readiness to focus effort on viable deals.
- **Account Coverage Planning**: Plans account touchpoints, territory focus, and contact strategy to sustain pipeline and revenue.
- **Proposal & Quotation Control**: Builds accurate proposals, pricing, and commercial terms aligned with customer needs and margin targets.
- **Sales Forecasting**: Maintains realistic revenue forecasts using deal stage evidence, timing, and conversion assumptions.
- **Contract & Compliance Review**: Checks terms, approvals, and policy requirements before closing to reduce legal and commercial risk.
