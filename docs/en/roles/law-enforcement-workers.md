## Core Skills

- **Incident Response**: Assesses threats, secures scenes, and applies lawful force and control measures during active incidents.
- **Criminal Investigation**: Collects facts, develops leads, and builds cases through interviews, evidence review, and investigative follow-up.
- **Evidence Handling**: Documents, preserves, and transfers evidence in a way that protects integrity and chain of custody.
- **Legal Procedure**: Executes stops, searches, arrests, and reports in compliance with statutory authority and procedural rules.
- **Patrol Risk Assessment**: Reads environments, identifies suspicious indicators, and adjusts patrol tactics to prevent escalation and harm.
