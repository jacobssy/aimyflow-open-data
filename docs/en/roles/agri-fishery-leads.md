## Core Skills

- **Production Planning**: Plans crop, livestock, or fishery cycles around seasons, capacity, labor, and market demand.
- **Resource Management**: Allocates land, water, feed, seed, equipment, and crews to keep operations efficient and on schedule.
- **Biosecurity Control**: Applies disease prevention, sanitation, and stock health measures to protect yields and product quality.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Ensures permits, environmental standards, traceability, and safety requirements are met across operations.
- **Harvest & Yield Assessment**: Monitors growth, harvest timing, catch volume, and output quality to improve returns and reduce losses.
