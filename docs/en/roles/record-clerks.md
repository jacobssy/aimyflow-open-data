## Core Skills

- **Records Classification**: Assigns records to the correct filing category, code, and retention group.
- **Indexing and Metadata**: Captures accurate names, dates, identifiers, and metadata for fast retrieval.
- **Records Retrieval**: Locates and issues requested files quickly while tracking their movement and return.
- **Retention Compliance**: Applies retention schedules, legal holds, and disposal rules to active and archived records.
- **Digital Records Processing**: Scans, verifies, and uploads documents with legible images and complete file data.
