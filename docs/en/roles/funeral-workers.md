## Core Skills

- **Body Preparation**: Cleans, dresses, and prepares the deceased respectfully according to family wishes and legal requirements.
- **Viewing Setup**: Sets up the viewing area, casket presentation, flowers, and memorial items for funeral services.
- **Funeral Logistics**: Coordinates transport, scheduling, cemetery or crematory bookings, and service timing across providers.
- **Death Documentation**: Completes permits, certificates, and case records accurately to meet funeral and burial regulations.
- **Infection Control**: Applies safe handling, sanitation, and protective procedures when working with remains and equipment.
