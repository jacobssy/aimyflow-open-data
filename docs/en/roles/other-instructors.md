## Core Skills

- **Lesson Planning**: Builds structured sessions with clear objectives, materials, timing, and practice activities.
- **Instruction Delivery**: Delivers instruction clearly and adapts explanations, demonstrations, and pacing to the audience.
- **Learner Assessment**: Assesses learner progress through tasks, observation, quizzes, or practical performance checks.
- **Content Adaptation**: Modifies teaching content for different levels, learning needs, formats, or instructional settings.
- **Training Records**: Maintains attendance, progress notes, results, and required instructional documentation accurately.
