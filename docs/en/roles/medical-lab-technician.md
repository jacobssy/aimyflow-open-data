## Core Skills

- **Specimen Processing**: Receives, labels, prepares, and routes patient specimens correctly to preserve sample integrity.
- **Instrument Operation**: Operates, calibrates, and maintains laboratory analyzers according to test protocols and manufacturer standards.
- **Quality Control Testing**: Runs control materials, reviews performance trends, and verifies that results meet quality criteria before release.
- **Result Validation**: Evaluates test results for plausibility, flags abnormalities, and escalates critical findings promptly.
- **Laboratory Safety Compliance**: Applies biosafety, infection control, and documentation requirements to meet clinical laboratory regulations.
