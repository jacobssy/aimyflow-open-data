## Core Skills

- **Curriculum Sequencing**: Plans coherent history units that align standards, chronology, and age-appropriate depth.
- **Source Analysis Instruction**: Teaches students to interpret primary and secondary sources, context, bias, and evidence.
- **Assessment Design**: Designs quizzes, essays, and document-based tasks that measure historical understanding accurately.
- **Historical Explanation**: Explains causes, consequences, continuity, and change in clear narratives students can follow.
- **Sensitive Topic Handling**: Handles war, identity, religion, and injustice topics with balance, accuracy, and classroom care.
