## Core Skills

- **Safe Vehicle Handling**: Operates the vehicle smoothly and safely across traffic, weather, and road conditions.
- **Route Execution**: Follows assigned routes and adjusts timing or sequence to maintain efficient operations.
- **Vehicle Inspection**: Performs pre-trip and post-trip checks to identify defects and ensure roadworthiness.
- **Transport Compliance**: Applies traffic laws, transport rules, and operating procedures throughout each trip.
- **Load and Trip Documentation**: Confirms load security and completes trip records, delivery notes, or operational logs accurately.
