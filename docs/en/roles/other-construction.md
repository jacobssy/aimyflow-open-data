## Core Skills

- **Site Preparation**: Prepares work areas, access routes, and temporary protections before construction starts.
- **Material Handling**: Moves, stores, and stages construction materials safely for efficient site operations.
- **Tool and Equipment Use**: Operates common construction tools and small equipment correctly for assigned tasks.
- **Safety Compliance**: Follows site safety rules, protective procedures, and hazard controls during daily work.
- **Construction Support Tasks**: Carries out basic construction support work such as cleanup, demolition assistance, and setup.
