## Core Skills

- **Site Coordination**: Coordinates crews, subcontractors, equipment, and access so daily site work stays aligned with the build plan.
- **Schedule Control**: Tracks progress against milestones, resolves delays, and updates look-ahead plans to keep the project on schedule.
- **Quality Inspection**: Verifies that workmanship, materials, and completed sections meet drawings, specifications, and acceptance standards.
- **Safety Compliance**: Enforces site safety rules, permits, and risk controls to prevent incidents during all phases of construction.
- **Drawing Review**: Reviews drawings and technical documents to identify conflicts, clarify details, and guide correct site execution.
