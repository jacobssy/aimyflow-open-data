## Core Skills

- **Microbial Culturing**: Cultivates, isolates, and maintains microorganisms under controlled laboratory conditions.
- **Microbial Identification**: Identifies microorganisms using microscopy, biochemical tests, and molecular methods.
- **Sterility and Contamination Control**: Prevents contamination and verifies aseptic technique throughout sampling and laboratory workflows.
- **Experimental Microbiology**: Designs and runs studies to measure microbial growth, behavior, and treatment response.
- **Laboratory Biosafety**: Applies biosafety procedures, waste handling rules, and documentation required for microbial work.
