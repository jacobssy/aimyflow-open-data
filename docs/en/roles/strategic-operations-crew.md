## Core Skills

- **Operational Planning**: Translate strategic goals into phased operating plans, owners, timelines, and measurable targets.
- **Performance Monitoring**: Track KPIs, detect variance early, and surface operational risks that affect strategic outcomes.
- **Cross-Functional Alignment**: Coordinate dependencies across teams so initiatives move on schedule and decisions stay aligned.
- **Process Governance**: Establish operating rules, review rhythms, and escalation paths that keep execution controlled and auditable.
- **Strategic Reporting**: Prepare decision-ready updates that link operational results, root causes, and recommended actions.
