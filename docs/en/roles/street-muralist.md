## Core Skills

- **Wall Surface Preparation**: Prepares exterior walls by cleaning, repairing, priming, and selecting coatings that hold up outdoors.
- **Large-Scale Composition**: Plans imagery for long sightlines, wall proportions, and urban context before painting begins.
- **Scale Transfer Methods**: Transfers sketches accurately onto large walls using grids, projection, or freehand layout.
- **Spray and Brush Control**: Controls line work, fills, fades, edges, and layering across spray paint and brush media.
- **Site Access and Safety**: Plans permits, access equipment, traffic exposure, and safe working practices for public-facing walls.
