## Core Skills

- **Legal Document Preparation**: Drafts, formats, and checks legal forms, correspondence, and supporting documents for accuracy and completeness.
- **Case File Management**: Maintains organized case files, tracks deadlines, and ensures documents are filed in the correct matter record.
- **Court Filing Procedures**: Prepares and submits filings according to court rules, deadlines, and required service procedures.
- **Legal Research Support**: Finds statutes, regulations, case references, and background materials to support legal work and document drafting.
- **Confidential Records Handling**: Handles sensitive client and case information in line with privacy rules, access controls, and office procedures.
