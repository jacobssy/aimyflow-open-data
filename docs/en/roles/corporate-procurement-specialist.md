## Core Skills

- **Sourcing Strategy**: Develops category-based sourcing plans aligned with demand, budget, and supply market conditions.
- **Supplier Evaluation**: Assesses suppliers on cost, quality, capacity, risk, and service before selection and renewal.
- **Contract Negotiation**: Negotiates pricing, terms, service levels, and commercial protections in supplier agreements.
- **Procurement Compliance**: Ensures purchasing activity follows internal policies, approval workflows, and regulatory requirements.
- **Spend Analysis**: Analyzes purchasing data to identify savings opportunities, demand patterns, and contract leakage.
