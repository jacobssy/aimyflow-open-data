## Core Skills

- **Archival Appraisal**: Evaluates museum records and object documentation to determine long-term archival value and retention.
- **Arrangement & Description**: Organizes fonds and creates finding aids, metadata, and catalog records that preserve provenance and context.
- **Preservation Management**: Monitors storage conditions and handling risks to protect fragile paper, photographic, and audiovisual archives.
- **Digital Archiving**: Manages digitization, file standards, and repository workflows to ensure reliable long-term access to records.
- **Access & Rights Control**: Applies access restrictions, copyright rules, and donor agreements when serving researchers and internal staff.
