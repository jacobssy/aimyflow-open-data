## Core Skills

- **Airway Support Setup**: Prepares and checks oxygen, aerosol, suction, and airway support equipment before patient use.
- **Patient Respiratory Monitoring**: Observes breathing pattern, oxygen saturation, and patient response during routine respiratory care.
- **Therapy Procedure Assistance**: Assists with prescribed respiratory treatments such as nebulization, chest therapy, and specimen collection.
- **Infection Control Practice**: Applies cleaning, disinfection, and isolation procedures for respiratory devices and patient contact.
- **Treatment Documentation**: Records treatment setup, patient tolerance, and equipment use accurately in the clinical chart.
