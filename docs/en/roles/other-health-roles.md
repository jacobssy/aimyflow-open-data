## Core Skills

- **Clinical Assessment**: Evaluates symptoms, history, and functional status to guide appropriate care actions.
- **Care Planning**: Builds practical care plans that match patient needs, goals, and service constraints.
- **Documentation Accuracy**: Maintains clear, accurate records that support continuity, billing, and legal compliance.
- **Safety Protocols**: Applies hygiene, risk control, and incident procedures to protect patients and staff.
- **Care Coordination**: Coordinates referrals, follow-up, and service handoffs across providers and care settings.
