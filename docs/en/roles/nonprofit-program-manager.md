## Core Skills

- **Program Design**: Designs service models, activities, and timelines that align community needs with mission goals.
- **Grant & Budget Management**: Tracks restricted funding, budgets, and spending rules to keep programs financially compliant and viable.
- **Outcome Measurement**: Defines indicators, collects program data, and evaluates outcomes for reporting and improvement.
- **Partner Coordination**: Coordinates community partners, vendors, and referral networks to deliver services reliably across cases and sites.
- **Safeguarding & Compliance**: Applies safeguarding, privacy, and reporting requirements to protect participants and meet regulatory obligations.
