## Core Skills

- **Mission Readiness**: Maintains personnel, equipment, and procedures in a state ready for assigned missions.
- **Field Operations**: Executes operational tasks in training or active environments under military procedures.
- **Equipment Handling**: Operates, checks, and safeguards military equipment according to technical and safety standards.
- **Rules Compliance**: Applies orders, security protocols, and legal requirements consistently in daily duties.
- **Situation Reporting**: Records and reports operational status, incidents, and observations accurately through command channels.
