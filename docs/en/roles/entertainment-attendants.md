## Core Skills

- **Guest Activity Facilitation**: Leads games and entertainment sessions while keeping guests engaged, safe, and on schedule.
- **Equipment Operation**: Sets up, operates, and checks entertainment equipment so each attraction runs reliably for guests.
- **Guest Safety Monitoring**: Monitors participant behavior, capacity limits, and venue conditions to prevent incidents during activities.
- **Queue and Flow Control**: Manages lines, entry timing, and guest circulation to keep entertainment areas orderly and efficient.
- **Venue Rules Enforcement**: Applies age, access, and conduct rules consistently to protect guests and maintain venue standards.
