## Core Skills

- **Environmental Sampling**: Collects air, water, soil, or waste samples using controlled field procedures and chain-of-custody rules.
- **Instrument Calibration**: Calibrates, checks, and maintains monitoring instruments so field and laboratory measurements stay reliable.
- **Laboratory Analysis**: Prepares samples and performs standard chemical or biological tests to identify pollutants and environmental conditions.
- **Data Interpretation**: Reviews monitoring results against baselines, trends, and thresholds to flag anomalies and support environmental assessments.
- **Regulatory Reporting**: Documents methods, results, and deviations in reports that meet permit, audit, and regulatory requirements.
