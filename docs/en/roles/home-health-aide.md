## Core Skills

- **Personal Care Assistance**: Supports bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, and mobility while preserving comfort and dignity.
- **Health Observation**: Monitors routine changes in condition and reports warning signs such as pain, confusion, or skin issues.
- **Safe Transfer Support**: Uses safe techniques and equipment to help clients move between bed, chair, toilet, and walking areas.
- **Nutrition and Meal Support**: Prepares suitable meals, assists with eating, and follows dietary needs or swallowing precautions.
- **Care Routine Documentation**: Records care provided, daily routines, and notable changes to support continuity and compliance.
