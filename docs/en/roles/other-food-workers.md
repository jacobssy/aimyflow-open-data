## Core Skills

- **Food Safety Handling**: Handles ingredients and prepared foods under strict hygiene, temperature, and contamination-control rules.
- **Food Preparation**: Prepares, portions, and assembles food items accurately to match product and service requirements.
- **Quality Inspection**: Checks appearance, freshness, texture, and labeling before products are served, packed, or shipped.
- **Equipment Sanitation**: Cleans and sanitizes tools, surfaces, and machines to maintain safe and efficient food operations.
- **Inventory Rotation**: Rotates stock by date and storage condition to reduce waste and keep food usable and compliant.
