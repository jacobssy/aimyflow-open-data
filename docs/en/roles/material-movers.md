## Core Skills

- **Load Handling**: Moves, lifts, and positions materials safely using proper handling methods and equipment.
- **Material Staging**: Stages materials in the correct area to support workflow, access, and site order.
- **Equipment Operation**: Operates pallet jacks, dollies, hoists, or conveyors according to task and load conditions.
- **Load Securing**: Secures items for movement or storage to prevent shifting, damage, and handling hazards.
- **Safety Compliance**: Follows lifting limits, route controls, and workplace safety procedures during material movement.
