## Core Skills

- **Therapeutic Exercise Delivery**: Leads prescribed exercise programs with correct technique, pacing, and patient support.
- **Mobility and Transfer Training**: Trains gait, balance, transfers, and functional mobility while maintaining patient safety.
- **Progress Monitoring**: Observes response to treatment and records functional changes for the supervising therapist.
- **Physical Agent Application**: Applies modalities such as heat, cold, electrical stimulation, or ultrasound as directed.
- **Rehabilitation Documentation**: Completes accurate treatment notes, exercise logs, and billing-related documentation in line with clinic standards.
