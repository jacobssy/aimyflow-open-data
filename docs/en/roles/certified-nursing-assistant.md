## Core Skills

- **Patient Hygiene Care**: Provides bathing, grooming, toileting, and other daily care while protecting comfort and dignity.
- **Mobility Assistance**: Assists with turning, transfers, walking, and positioning to reduce fall and pressure injury risk.
- **Vital Signs Monitoring**: Measures and records temperature, pulse, respiration, blood pressure, and other assigned observations accurately.
- **Patient Observation**: Recognizes changes in condition such as pain, confusion, skin issues, or intake and reports them promptly.
- **Infection Control Practice**: Follows hand hygiene, PPE, isolation, and cleaning procedures to prevent transmission in care settings.
