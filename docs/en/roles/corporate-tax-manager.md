## Core Skills

- **Tax Provision Reporting**: Prepares current and deferred tax calculations for accurate financial reporting under applicable standards.
- **Corporate Tax Compliance**: Manages corporate income tax returns, estimated payments, and filing calendars across entities and jurisdictions.
- **Tax Planning**: Evaluates transaction structures and business changes to improve tax efficiency while controlling technical risk.
- **Transfer Pricing Oversight**: Oversees intercompany pricing policies, documentation, and adjustments to support arm’s-length compliance.
- **Tax Audit Defense**: Handles tax authority inquiries, audit support, and position documentation to resolve disputes efficiently.
