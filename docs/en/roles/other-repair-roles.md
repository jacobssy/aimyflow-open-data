## Core Skills

- **Fault Diagnosis**: Identifies root causes through inspection, testing, and symptom analysis before any repair begins.
- **Component Repair**: Restores or replaces damaged parts using appropriate repair methods, tools, and materials.
- **Equipment Testing**: Verifies that repaired equipment operates safely and meets functional performance requirements.
- **Service Documentation**: Records findings, repair actions, parts used, and test results for traceability and follow-up.
- **Repair Safety**: Applies isolation, handling, and workplace safety procedures to prevent injury and equipment damage.
