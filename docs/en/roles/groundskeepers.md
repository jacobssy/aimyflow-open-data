## Core Skills

- **Turf Maintenance**: Maintains healthy turf through mowing, edging, fertilizing, aeration, and seasonal treatment schedules.
- **Plant Care**: Monitors trees, shrubs, and flower beds for pruning needs, plant health, and proper watering.
- **Irrigation Upkeep**: Inspects and adjusts irrigation systems to ensure efficient coverage, correct timing, and leak prevention.
- **Equipment Operation**: Operates and maintains mowers, trimmers, blowers, and related equipment safely and reliably.
- **Site Cleanliness**: Keeps grounds clean and presentable by removing debris, clearing paths, and responding to seasonal conditions.
