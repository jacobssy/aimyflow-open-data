## Core Skills

- **Client Needs Assessment**: Assesses personal, family, and social risks to determine service eligibility and support priorities.
- **Care Plan Development**: Builds structured service plans with goals, interventions, timelines, and follow-up actions.
- **Resource Coordination**: Connects clients with housing, healthcare, financial aid, legal support, and community services.
- **Case Documentation**: Maintains accurate case notes, service records, and reports that meet legal and funding requirements.
- **Safeguarding Intervention**: Identifies abuse, neglect, or crisis situations and initiates mandated protection and referral steps.
