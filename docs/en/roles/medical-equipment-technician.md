## Core Skills

- **Device Installation**: Installs medical devices, verifies site readiness, and completes functional setup for clinical use.
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Performs scheduled inspections, calibration checks, and part replacement to keep equipment reliable.
- **Fault Diagnosis**: Troubleshoots electrical, mechanical, and software faults and restores safe equipment operation.
- **Safety Compliance**: Checks equipment against safety standards, test protocols, and hospital compliance requirements.
- **Service Documentation**: Maintains accurate service records, test results, and maintenance histories for traceability and audits.
