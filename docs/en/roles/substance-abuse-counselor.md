## Core Skills

- **Addiction Assessment**: Evaluates substance use patterns, co-occurring risks, and readiness for treatment using structured clinical methods.
- **Treatment Planning**: Builds individualized recovery plans with measurable goals, service coordination, and clear review points.
- **Relapse Prevention**: Teaches clients to identify triggers, manage cravings, and use coping strategies to sustain recovery.
- **Crisis Intervention**: Responds to overdose risk, withdrawal complications, and acute safety concerns with timely clinical action.
- **Clinical Documentation**: Maintains accurate treatment notes, progress records, and compliance documentation for clinical and legal standards.
