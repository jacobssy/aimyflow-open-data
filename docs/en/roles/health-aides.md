## Core Skills

- **Personal Care Support**: Assists with bathing, dressing, toileting, and grooming while preserving comfort and dignity.
- **Mobility Assistance**: Supports safe transfers, walking, and positioning to reduce fall and pressure injury risks.
- **Health Observation**: Monitors daily condition changes and promptly reports pain, confusion, skin issues, or appetite loss.
- **Infection Control**: Follows hygiene, glove, cleaning, and isolation procedures to protect patients and care environments.
- **Care Documentation**: Records care provided, intake, output, and notable events accurately for continuity of care.
