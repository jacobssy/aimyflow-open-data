## Core Skills

- **Operational Planning**: Builds operating plans that align staffing, capacity, timelines, and service targets.
- **Process Governance**: Standardizes procedures, controls execution, and corrects deviations across specialized operations.
- **Performance Monitoring**: Tracks operational metrics, analyzes trends, and drives actions to improve output and reliability.
- **Compliance Oversight**: Ensures operations meet regulatory, safety, quality, and internal control requirements.
- **Resource Coordination**: Allocates people, vendors, equipment, and budgets to keep specialized operations running effectively.
