## Core Skills

- **Preventive Maintenance Planning**: Builds and adjusts maintenance schedules to reduce failures and downtime.
- **Fault Diagnosis**: Leads technical troubleshooting to identify root causes and restore equipment safely.
- **Work Order Control**: Prioritizes, assigns, and follows up work orders to keep maintenance execution on track.
- **Safety and Compliance Control**: Enforces lockout, permits, and maintenance standards to meet safety and regulatory requirements.
- **Spare Parts Management**: Controls critical spare parts, stock levels, and replenishment to support reliable maintenance.
