## Core Skills

- **Artist Booking**: Secures artists by aligning fees, routing, availability, and contractual terms with the event plan.
- **Show Budgeting**: Builds and controls the event budget across talent, production, venue, marketing, and margin targets.
- **Venue & Routing Planning**: Selects viable venues and dates by balancing market demand, technical fit, calendar holds, and tour routing.
- **Ticketing Strategy**: Sets pricing, onsale timing, inventory releases, and sales pacing to maximize attendance and revenue.
- **Event Compliance**: Ensures permits, insurance, safety plans, licensing, and local regulations are fully covered before show day.
