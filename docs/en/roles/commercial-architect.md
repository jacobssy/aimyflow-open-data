## Core Skills

- **Program Planning**: Defines tenant, customer, and operational space needs into a viable commercial building brief.
- **Retail Space Planning**: Plans circulation, frontage, and unit layout to support trading performance and customer flow.
- **Code Compliance**: Develops designs that satisfy fire safety, accessibility, zoning, and commercial occupancy regulations.
- **Façade and Branding Integration**: Coordinates façade design, signage, and material expression with tenant identity and planning controls.
- **Construction Documentation**: Produces coordinated drawing packages and details for permitting, tendering, and commercial fit-out delivery.
