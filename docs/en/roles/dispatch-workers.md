## Core Skills

- **Route Scheduling**: Builds efficient daily dispatch schedules based on orders, locations, timing, and vehicle capacity.
- **Load Allocation**: Assigns loads to vehicles and drivers to maximize utilization while meeting service constraints.
- **Live Dispatch Control**: Monitors active trips and adjusts dispatch plans quickly when delays, breakdowns, or priority changes occur.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Checks dispatch decisions against driving hours, safety rules, delivery restrictions, and documentation requirements.
- **Dispatch Systems Use**: Uses dispatch software, GPS tracking, and order data to keep schedules, statuses, and records accurate.
