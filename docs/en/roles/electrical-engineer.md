## Core Skills

- **Circuit Design**: Designs analog, digital, or power circuits that meet electrical, thermal, and performance targets.
- **Power Systems Analysis**: Calculates loads, faults, protection settings, and distribution behavior for safe and reliable operation.
- **Electrical Testing**: Validates equipment with measurements, test plans, and troubleshooting to confirm performance and safety.
- **Standards Compliance**: Applies electrical codes, safety standards, and documentation practices required for approval and deployment.
- **Control Systems Integration**: Integrates sensors, actuators, drives, and PLC-based controls into functioning electrical systems.
