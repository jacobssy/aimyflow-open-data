## Core Skills

- **Media Strategy**: Builds channel plans that align messages, audiences, timing, and campaign objectives.
- **Press Relations**: Manages journalist outreach, pitching, follow-up, and press material distribution.
- **Message Development**: Shapes clear narratives, key messages, and spokesperson lines for each audience and outlet.
- **Content Production**: Produces press releases, media kits, statements, and digital content in publication-ready form.
- **Media Monitoring**: Tracks coverage, sentiment, reach, and issue signals to evaluate impact and inform response.
