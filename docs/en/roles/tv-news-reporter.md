## Core Skills

- **Story Verification**: Confirms facts, sources, and visuals before broadcast under tight deadlines.
- **Live Field Reporting**: Delivers clear live updates from the scene while adapting to fast-changing events.
- **Interviewing on Camera**: Asks precise on-camera questions that elicit usable, newsworthy responses.
- **Broadcast Script Writing**: Writes concise broadcast scripts matched to visuals, timing, and editorial angle.
- **Editorial News Judgment**: Evaluates what is newsworthy, accurate, and appropriate for broadcast and audience impact.
