## Core Skills

- **Ingredient Preparation**: Prepares, weighs, trims, and stages raw ingredients to match production specifications.
- **Process Control**: Monitors time, temperature, mixing, and flow settings to keep processing within target limits.
- **Food Safety Compliance**: Applies hygiene, allergen, and contamination controls in line with food safety procedures and standards.
- **Quality Inspection**: Checks product appearance, texture, weight, and packaging to identify defects before release.
- **Equipment Sanitation**: Cleans and sanitizes processing equipment and work areas to support safe and efficient production.
