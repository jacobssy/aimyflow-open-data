## Core Skills

- **Route Planning**: Builds efficient delivery schedules based on volume, distance, timing, and fleet capacity.
- **Fleet Coordination**: Assigns vehicles and drivers to daily transport demands while balancing utilization and service levels.
- **Transport Compliance**: Ensures transport operations meet safety rules, driver-hour limits, and required documentation standards.
- **Shipment Monitoring**: Tracks shipments in transit, manages delays, and updates stakeholders on delivery status changes.
- **Transport Cost Control**: Monitors route, fuel, subcontracting, and loading costs to keep transport within budget.
