## Core Skills

- **Lesson Planning**: Designs structured lessons with clear objectives, pacing, materials, and age-appropriate activities.
- **Classroom Management**: Maintains an orderly, inclusive learning environment through routines, behavior guidance, and smooth transitions.
- **Student Assessment**: Uses quizzes, assignments, observation, and feedback to measure progress and adjust instruction.
- **Differentiated Instruction**: Adapts content, support, and task difficulty to meet varied learning needs within one class.
- **Curriculum Delivery**: Delivers required curriculum standards while sequencing units, tracking coverage, and preparing students for exams.
