## Core Skills

- **Signal Fault Diagnosis**: Locates communication faults by testing signal levels, continuity, and equipment response.
- **Cable and Line Repair**: Repairs damaged cables, connectors, and line sections to restore stable transmission.
- **Equipment Replacement**: Removes faulty communication units and installs compatible replacements with correct settings.
- **Network Restoration Testing**: Verifies restored service through functional checks, transmission tests, and circuit validation.
- **Repair Safety Compliance**: Performs repair work in line with electrical safety rules, isolation procedures, and site requirements.
