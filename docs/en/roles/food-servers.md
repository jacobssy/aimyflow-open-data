## Core Skills

- **Order Taking**: Accurately records guest orders, modifiers, and dietary needs for correct kitchen preparation.
- **Table Service**: Serves food and beverages in the right sequence while maintaining table readiness throughout the meal.
- **Menu Knowledge**: Explains dishes, ingredients, allergens, and pairings so guests can order with confidence.
- **POS & Billing**: Processes orders, split checks, and payments correctly in the POS with minimal errors.
- **Food Safety**: Follows hygiene, allergen, and handling procedures that protect guests and meet service standards.
