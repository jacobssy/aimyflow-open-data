## Core Skills

- **User Research**: Translates user behavior, context, and pain points into actionable product requirements.
- **Concept Development**: Builds product concepts that align function, form, brand intent, and market position.
- **CAD Modeling**: Creates precise 3D models and surfaces suitable for engineering review and prototyping.
- **Materials & Manufacturing**: Selects materials and production processes that balance aesthetics, durability, cost, and scalability.
- **Prototype Evaluation**: Tests prototypes to refine usability, proportions, construction details, and production readiness.
