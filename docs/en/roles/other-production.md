## Core Skills

- **Process Operation**: Run production steps to standard settings, timing, and output requirements.
- **Equipment Setup**: Prepare, change over, and fine-tune equipment for different products or batches.
- **In-Process Quality Control**: Check measurements, appearance, or test results and act on deviations during production.
- **Production Documentation**: Record batches, settings, material usage, and incidents to maintain traceability.
- **Safety and GMP Compliance**: Follow safety, hygiene, and site procedures while handling materials and equipment.
