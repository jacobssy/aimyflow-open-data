## Core Skills

- **Workplace Investigations**: Investigates complaints impartially, documents findings, and recommends defensible actions.
- **Labor Law Compliance**: Applies labor law and company policy consistently across disciplinary and workplace matters.
- **Grievance Resolution**: Resolves employee grievances through structured review, mediation, and timely case closure.
- **Disciplinary Process Management**: Guides fair disciplinary processes with clear evidence, due process, and proper documentation.
- **Employee Relations Risk Analysis**: Identifies patterns in cases and workforce data to prevent escalation and reduce legal risk.
