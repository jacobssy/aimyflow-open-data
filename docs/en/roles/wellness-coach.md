## Core Skills

- **Health Assessment**: Evaluates lifestyle habits, stress, sleep, and health history to identify coaching priorities.
- **Behavior Change Planning**: Builds realistic step-by-step plans that turn wellness goals into sustainable daily routines.
- **Habit Tracking**: Monitors adherence, outcomes, and barriers over time to adjust coaching interventions.
- **Nutrition & Lifestyle Guidance**: Provides practical guidance on nutrition, movement, recovery, and stress-management habits within scope.
- **Scope & Referral Management**: Recognizes when client needs exceed coaching scope and refers appropriately to licensed professionals.
