## Core Skills

- **Operational Planning**: Translate business goals into staffing, timelines, budgets, and execution priorities.
- **Performance Oversight**: Track operational results through KPIs, variance reviews, and corrective action plans.
- **Policy & Compliance Control**: Maintain procedures and controls that keep operations aligned with internal policy and external rules.
- **Cross-Functional Coordination**: Align dependencies, approvals, and handoffs across teams to keep work moving on schedule.
- **Risk & Issue Management**: Identify operational risks early, assess impact, and drive mitigation through to resolution.
