## Core Skills

- **Performance Delivery**: Delivers polished live or recorded performances while maintaining timing, energy, and audience engagement.
- **Technical Routine Execution**: Executes sport-specific or performance-specific techniques accurately, consistently, and under pressure.
- **Training & Conditioning**: Plans and follows structured practice, recovery, and conditioning routines to sustain performance readiness.
- **Event & Competition Preparation**: Prepares schedules, rehearsals, equipment, and logistics to be ready for events, matches, or shows.
- **Safety & Rules Compliance**: Works within safety protocols, event rules, and conduct standards to reduce risk and avoid penalties.
