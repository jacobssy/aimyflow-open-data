## Core Skills

- **Listening Analysis**: Evaluates recordings for melody, rhythm, arrangement, and overall sonic character.
- **Genre Knowledge**: Identifies genre traits, key artists, and stylistic influences across music scenes.
- **Playlist Curation**: Builds playlists with coherent flow, mood, pacing, and audience fit.
- **Release Tracking**: Monitors new releases, reissues, and artist activity to stay current and informed.
- **Audio Format Literacy**: Understands common audio formats, mastering differences, and playback quality considerations.
