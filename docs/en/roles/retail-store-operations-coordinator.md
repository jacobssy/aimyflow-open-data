## Core Skills

- **Inventory Control**: Monitors stock levels, coordinates replenishment, and resolves discrepancies to keep shelves available and accurate.
- **Store Process Scheduling**: Builds daily operational schedules for deliveries, floor tasks, promotions, and peak trading periods.
- **Visual Merchandising Execution**: Implements planograms and promotional setups to meet brand standards and commercial priorities.
- **Operational Compliance**: Checks store procedures, safety routines, and documentation to meet company and regulatory requirements.
- **Sales Performance Tracking**: Tracks daily sales, conversion, and shrink indicators to adjust store actions and improve results.
