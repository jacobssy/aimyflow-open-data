## Core Skills

- **Mathematical Modeling**: Formulate real-world systems as tractable mathematical models with explicit assumptions and limits.
- **Analytical Derivation**: Derive governing equations, approximations, and closed-form results to explain system behavior.
- **Numerical Methods**: Design and assess numerical schemes for simulation, optimization, or solving equations at scale.
- **Model Validation**: Test model predictions against data, quantify error, and identify breakdown conditions.
- **Optimization Formulation**: Translate operational objectives and constraints into optimization problems that can be solved reliably.
