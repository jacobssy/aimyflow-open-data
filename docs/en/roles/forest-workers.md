## Core Skills

- **Tree Felling**: Fell trees safely with controlled cuts based on lean, diameter, and surrounding hazards.
- **Chainsaw Operation**: Operate and maintain chainsaws for cutting, limbing, and bucking under field conditions.
- **Timber Assessment**: Assess tree species, quality, and harvest value to support cutting and sorting decisions.
- **Harvest Planning**: Plan extraction routes, work sequence, and landing areas to move timber efficiently and safely.
- **Forest Safety Compliance**: Apply forestry safety procedures, protective equipment, and site controls in hazardous terrain.
