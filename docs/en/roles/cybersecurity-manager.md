## Core Skills

- **Security Strategy**: Defines security priorities, target controls, and investment plans aligned with business risk.
- **Risk Assessment**: Assesses threats, vulnerabilities, and business impact to prioritize mitigation actions.
- **Incident Response**: Directs preparation, escalation, containment, and post-incident review for security events.
- **Security Governance**: Maintains policies, control ownership, metrics, and reporting across the security program.
- **Compliance Oversight**: Ensures security controls meet regulatory, audit, and contractual requirements.
