## Core Skills

- **Episode Development**: Shapes episode concepts, structures, and guest angles to fit the show’s audience and editorial goals.
- **Recording Production**: Runs recording sessions by coordinating hosts, guests, briefs, timing, and technical readiness.
- **Audio Post-Production**: Edits dialogue, pacing, music, and sound treatment into a clean and publish-ready episode.
- **Publishing Workflow**: Prepares metadata, show notes, assets, and platform scheduling to release episodes accurately and on time.
- **Rights & Clearance**: Checks music, clips, guest releases, and usage permissions so episodes meet legal and platform requirements.
