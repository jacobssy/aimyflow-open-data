## Core Skills

- **Fault Isolation**: Pinpoints failures in miniature or high-tolerance assemblies through systematic inspection and testing.
- **Micro-Component Repair**: Repairs delicate parts using precision hand tools, fine adjustment, and controlled handling techniques.
- **Calibration and Alignment**: Restores instruments or mechanisms to exact operating tolerances through calibration, alignment, and adjustment.
- **Measurement Verification**: Verifies dimensions, fit, and performance with gauges, microscopes, and other precision measuring equipment.
- **Service Documentation**: Records defects, repair steps, replaced parts, and final test results to maintain traceable service history.
