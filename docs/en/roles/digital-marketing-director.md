## Core Skills

- **Channel Strategy**: Defines the role, budget, and targets of each digital channel to drive efficient growth.
- **Campaign Performance Management**: Monitors spend, bidding, creatives, and audience signals to improve campaign ROI at scale.
- **Attribution & Analytics**: Builds measurement frameworks that connect media activity to pipeline, revenue, and lifetime value.
- **Lifecycle Automation**: Designs automated journeys for acquisition, nurturing, retention, and reactivation across digital touchpoints.
- **Martech & Data Governance**: Oversees platform integration, tracking quality, consent controls, and data standards for reliable execution.
