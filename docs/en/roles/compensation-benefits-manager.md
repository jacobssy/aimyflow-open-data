## Core Skills

- **Compensation Benchmarking**: Analyzes market pay data to position salary ranges competitively and consistently.
- **Job Evaluation**: Evaluates roles using grading frameworks to maintain internal equity across the organization.
- **Benefits Program Design**: Designs and updates benefits programs that balance employee needs, cost, and policy goals.
- **Payroll & Benefits Compliance**: Ensures pay and benefits practices comply with tax, labor, and reporting requirements.
- **Incentive Plan Management**: Administers bonus and incentive plans, including eligibility rules, calculations, and payout controls.
