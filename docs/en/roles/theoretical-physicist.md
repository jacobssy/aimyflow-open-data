## Core Skills

- **Mathematical Modeling**: Formulates physical systems into tractable equations, approximations, and boundary conditions.
- **Analytical Derivation**: Derives rigorous results from first principles using symmetry, conservation laws, and formal methods.
- **Computational Simulation**: Implements numerical methods to test theories where closed-form solutions are unavailable.
- **Model Validation**: Checks consistency against limiting cases, known results, and relevant experimental or observational data.
- **Theory Development**: Builds and refines theoretical frameworks that yield testable predictions and clear domains of validity.
