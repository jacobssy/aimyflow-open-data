## Core Skills

- **Script Interpretation**: Analyzes script intent, subtext, and character context to shape a believable vocal performance.
- **Character Voice Control**: Adjusts tone, texture, age, and emotional range to create distinct and consistent character voices.
- **Timing and Lip Sync**: Matches phrasing and pacing to animation, dubbing cues, or edited timing without losing performance quality.
- **Microphone Technique**: Controls distance, projection, breath, and plosives to capture clean, usable takes in studio sessions.
- **Session Direction Response**: Adapts performance quickly from live direction and retake notes while maintaining character continuity.
