## Core Skills

- **Workforce Planning**: Translates business growth plans into hiring demand, priorities, and realistic recruiting capacity.
- **Sourcing Strategy**: Builds channel mix, talent pipelines, and outreach approaches for hard-to-fill and high-volume roles.
- **Selection Design**: Designs structured interviews and assessment criteria that improve hiring quality and reduce bias.
- **Offer Management**: Shapes competitive offers and closing plans using market pay data, candidate drivers, and approval controls.
- **Recruiting Analytics**: Tracks funnel metrics, time-to-fill, and source performance to improve forecasting and process decisions.
