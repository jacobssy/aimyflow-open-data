## Core Skills

- **Story Architecture**: Designs plot, pacing, and turning points into a coherent long-form narrative.
- **Character Development**: Builds distinctive characters with believable motives, voice, and emotional arcs.
- **Narrative Voice**: Controls style, point of view, and tone to sustain the desired reading experience.
- **Worldbuilding**: Establishes settings, rules, and background details that support the story consistently.
- **Manuscript Revision**: Reworks drafts to sharpen structure, prose, continuity, and scene-level impact.
