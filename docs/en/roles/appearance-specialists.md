## Core Skills

- **Client Image Assessment**: Evaluates facial features, body proportions, lifestyle, and goals to define a suitable appearance direction.
- **Color Analysis**: Identifies the tones that best complement the client's skin, hair, eyes, and overall visual balance.
- **Styling Planning**: Builds coordinated outfits, grooming choices, and accessory plans for daily, professional, or special contexts.
- **Wardrobe Editing**: Reviews existing clothing to remove mismatches, identify gaps, and improve outfit versatility.
- **Product Recommendation**: Selects clothing, cosmetics, haircare, or grooming products that fit the client's needs, budget, and upkeep level.
