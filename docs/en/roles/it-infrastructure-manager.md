## Core Skills

- **Infrastructure Architecture**: Designs scalable compute, storage, network, and platform foundations for business services.
- **Operations Reliability**: Maintains uptime through monitoring, capacity planning, patching, backup, and incident control.
- **Cloud & Data Center Management**: Runs hybrid infrastructure across cloud and on-prem environments with consistent service levels.
- **Security & Compliance Controls**: Implements access, hardening, logging, and audit controls to meet security and regulatory requirements.
- **Disaster Recovery Planning**: Defines recovery strategies, failover procedures, and test plans to restore critical services quickly.
