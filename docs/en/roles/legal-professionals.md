## Core Skills

- **Legal Research**: Researches statutes, regulations, precedent, and guidance to support legal advice and case strategy.
- **Contract Drafting**: Drafts, reviews, and negotiates contract terms to allocate risk and protect client interests.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Assesses legal obligations and designs controls, policies, and filings to meet regulatory requirements.
- **Litigation Management**: Builds case positions, manages filings and evidence, and coordinates procedure through hearings or settlement.
- **Legal Risk Analysis**: Evaluates exposure, enforcement risk, and legal options to guide decisions in transactions and disputes.
