## Core Skills

- **Pediatric Assessment**: Evaluates growth, development, vital signs, and age-specific symptoms to identify pediatric conditions early.
- **Vaccination Management**: Plans immunization schedules, checks contraindications, and monitors vaccine safety and follow-up.
- **Acute Pediatric Care**: Diagnoses and treats common urgent illnesses in infants, children, and adolescents with age-appropriate decisions.
- **Chronic Condition Follow-up**: Monitors long-term conditions such as asthma or diabetes and adjusts treatment as the child grows.
- **Developmental Screening**: Screens for developmental, behavioral, and learning concerns and coordinates timely referral when needed.
