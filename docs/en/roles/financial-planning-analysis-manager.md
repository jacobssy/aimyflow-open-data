## Core Skills

- **Budget Planning**: Builds annual budgets and rolling plans aligned to business targets and operating constraints.
- **Forecasting**: Produces monthly forecasts by updating revenue, cost, cash, and headcount assumptions.
- **Variance Analysis**: Explains actual-versus-plan results and identifies the business drivers behind performance gaps.
- **Management Reporting**: Delivers KPI reports and decision-ready insights for month-end, reviews, and executive meetings.
- **Business Case Modeling**: Evaluates investments and strategic initiatives through scenario models, returns analysis, and risk assumptions.
