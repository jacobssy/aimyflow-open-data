## Core Skills

- **Experimental Design**: Designs controlled studies with appropriate variables, sample sizes, and endpoints to test biological hypotheses reliably.
- **Laboratory Methods**: Executes and adapts assays, cultures, extractions, and instrument-based protocols with technical precision.
- **Biological Data Analysis**: Interprets experimental and omics data using statistical and computational methods to draw defensible conclusions.
- **Biosafety & Compliance**: Maintains safe, traceable research practices that meet biosafety, ethical, and regulatory requirements.
- **Scientific Documentation**: Documents methods, observations, and results in reproducible records, reports, and publication-ready formats.
