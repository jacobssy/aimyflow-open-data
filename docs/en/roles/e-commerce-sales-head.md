## Core Skills

- **Marketplace Strategy**: Builds channel plans by marketplace, balancing growth, pricing, margin, and brand positioning.
- **Sales Forecasting**: Translates traffic, conversion, seasonality, and inventory signals into actionable revenue forecasts.
- **Promotional Planning**: Designs campaign calendars, offer mechanics, and budget allocation to maximize peak-period sales.
- **Assortment & Pricing**: Shapes product mix and price ladders using demand, competitor, and margin data.
- **Platform Compliance**: Ensures listings, promotions, and order operations meet platform rules and commercial standards.
