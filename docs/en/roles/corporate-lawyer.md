## Core Skills

- **Contract Drafting**: Drafts, reviews, and negotiates commercial agreements that protect the company’s legal and business position.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Interprets applicable laws and designs controls to keep business operations compliant across jurisdictions.
- **Corporate Governance**: Supports boards, shareholders, and management with resolutions, records, and governance procedures.
- **M&A Due Diligence**: Identifies legal risks, deal breakers, and post-closing obligations in acquisitions, investments, and restructurings.
- **Dispute Risk Assessment**: Assesses exposure from claims, investigations, and disputes and advises on defensible legal positions.
