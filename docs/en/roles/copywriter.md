## Core Skills

- **Message Strategy**: Shapes core messaging from audience insight, brand positioning, and campaign objectives.
- **Headline Writing**: Writes clear, persuasive headlines that capture attention and frame the offer instantly.
- **Channel Adaptation**: Adapts copy to format, platform behavior, and funnel stage across digital and print channels.
- **Brand Voice Control**: Maintains a consistent brand voice while tailoring language to product, audience, and market.
- **Copy Review**: Reviews copy for clarity, claims accuracy, legal risk, grammar, and readiness for release.
