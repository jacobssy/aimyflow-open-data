## Core Skills

- **Production Scheduling**: Builds realistic production schedules around demand, capacity, staffing, and material availability.
- **Shop Floor Coordination**: Directs daily shop floor execution to keep lines running safely, steadily, and on target.
- **Quality Control Oversight**: Monitors process quality, addresses deviations, and ensures output meets specification before release.
- **Resource Allocation**: Balances labor, equipment, and materials across shifts to prevent bottlenecks and downtime.
- **Safety and Compliance**: Enforces safety rules, operating procedures, and audit requirements throughout production activities.
