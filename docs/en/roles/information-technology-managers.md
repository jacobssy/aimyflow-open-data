## Core Skills

- **IT Strategy Planning**: Aligns technology roadmaps, budgets, and priorities with business goals and operating needs.
- **Infrastructure Oversight**: Directs the availability, capacity, and lifecycle management of networks, servers, cloud, and end-user systems.
- **Cybersecurity Governance**: Establishes security policies, access controls, risk treatment, and incident readiness across the IT environment.
- **Vendor and Budget Control**: Manages technology sourcing, contract performance, cost control, and investment decisions for IT services and platforms.
- **Service Delivery Management**: Runs support operations, service levels, change processes, and issue escalation to maintain reliable IT services.
