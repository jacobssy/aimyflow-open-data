## Core Skills

- **Mechanical Design**: Develops components and assemblies that meet functional, manufacturing, and service requirements.
- **CAD Modeling**: Builds accurate 3D models and drawings with tolerances, fits, and production details.
- **Engineering Analysis**: Evaluates loads, stresses, motion, and thermal behavior to verify design performance.
- **Materials Selection**: Selects materials and finishes based on strength, wear, environment, cost, and manufacturability.
- **Testing & Validation**: Plans tests, analyzes results, and resolves deviations before release to production.
