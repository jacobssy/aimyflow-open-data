## Core Skills

- **Asset Allocation**: Sets portfolio weights across asset classes to match mandate, risk budget, and market outlook.
- **Security Selection**: Evaluates securities using fundamental, valuation, and market data to build high-conviction positions.
- **Risk Management**: Monitors factor, liquidity, concentration, and drawdown risks to keep the portfolio within limits.
- **Portfolio Rebalancing**: Executes trades and rebalancing plans to maintain target exposures while managing costs and liquidity.
- **Mandate Compliance**: Ensures holdings, trades, and reporting comply with client guidelines, regulations, and internal controls.
