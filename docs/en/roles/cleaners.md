## Core Skills

- **Surface Sanitization**: Cleans and disinfects floors, touchpoints, and work surfaces using the right products and methods.
- **Chemical Handling**: Dilutes, stores, and applies cleaning chemicals safely according to labels, risks, and surface type.
- **Equipment Operation**: Operates vacuums, scrubbers, mops, and other tools efficiently while maintaining them for reliable use.
- **Waste Management**: Collects, sorts, and disposes of waste correctly, including recyclable and potentially hazardous materials.
- **Cleaning Schedule Execution**: Follows task schedules and area standards to complete routine and deep cleaning on time.
