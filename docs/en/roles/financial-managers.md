## Core Skills

- **Financial Planning**: Builds budgets, forecasts, and funding plans aligned with business targets.
- **Financial Analysis**: Evaluates performance, profitability, and cash flow to support management decisions.
- **Financial Reporting**: Produces accurate financial statements and management reports on schedule.
- **Compliance Control**: Maintains controls and accounting practices that meet regulatory and audit requirements.
- **Capital Management**: Manages liquidity, financing, and capital allocation to sustain operations and growth.
