## Core Skills

- **Radio Setup**: Configures communication equipment, channels, frequencies, and encryption settings for operational use.
- **Signal Monitoring**: Monitors signal quality, interference, and transmission status to keep links stable.
- **Fault Isolation**: Identifies equipment, cabling, power, or network faults and restores service quickly.
- **Link Testing**: Performs transmission tests, coverage checks, and acceptance verification after setup or maintenance.
- **Operational Logging**: Records configurations, incidents, maintenance actions, and handover details for traceable operations.
