## Core Skills

- **Threat Assessment**: Evaluates site, event, and personnel risks to set protection priorities and control measures.
- **Security Operations Planning**: Designs guard coverage, post orders, patrol patterns, and escalation paths for each assignment.
- **Incident Command**: Directs real-time response to breaches, disturbances, and emergencies while coordinating resources on scene.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Ensures protective services follow licensing rules, use-of-force standards, reporting duties, and site policies.
- **Personnel Readiness**: Verifies staff readiness through briefing quality, equipment checks, qualification tracking, and post-performance review.
