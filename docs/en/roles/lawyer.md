## Core Skills

- **Legal Research**: Finds statutes, precedents, and commentary that directly support legal advice and case strategy.
- **Contract Drafting**: Drafts and revises contracts with precise terms, risk allocation, and enforceable obligations.
- **Case Analysis**: Assesses facts, evidence, and legal issues to determine claims, defenses, and litigation exposure.
- **Court Filings**: Prepares pleadings, motions, and procedural filings that meet court rules and deadlines.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Reviews business activities against applicable regulations and advises on compliant courses of action.
