## Core Skills

- **Artistic Research**: Researches references, context, and source material to inform artistic direction and content.
- **Concept Development**: Shapes themes, visual ideas, or interpretive approaches into a coherent artistic concept.
- **Production Planning**: Plans schedules, materials, space, and technical needs to deliver artistic work reliably.
- **Media & Technique Selection**: Chooses suitable media, tools, and methods based on intent, constraints, and presentation format.
- **Exhibition & Presentation Standards**: Prepares work for display with attention to installation, documentation, handling, and audience experience.
