## Core Skills

- **Drilling Operations**: Operates drill rigs to bore blast holes accurately to the planned depth and pattern.
- **Ground Support**: Installs bolts, mesh, and supports to stabilize rock and keep underground workings safe.
- **Material Extraction**: Loads and moves ore or waste efficiently while following production targets and haul routes.
- **Mine Equipment Operation**: Handles loaders, haul trucks, and other mining machines safely in confined and hazardous areas.
- **Safety and Gas Checks**: Performs pre-shift inspections, gas testing, and hazard checks before work begins.
