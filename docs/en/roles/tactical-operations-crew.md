## Core Skills

- **Mission Planning**: Builds actionable mission plans around objectives, terrain, timing, and threat factors.
- **Threat Assessment**: Assesses hostile activity, vulnerabilities, and escalation risks to guide tactical decisions.
- **Tactical Movement**: Executes movement, positioning, and cover techniques safely in dynamic operational environments.
- **Rules of Engagement**: Applies force, detention, and search procedures within legal and operational constraints.
- **Equipment Readiness**: Maintains weapons, protective gear, communications, and mission kits at operational readiness.
