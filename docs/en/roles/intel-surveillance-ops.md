## Core Skills

- **Target Monitoring**: Maintains continuous watch on persons, vehicles, sites, or signals and records relevant activity accurately.
- **Intelligence Assessment**: Evaluates observations and source inputs to judge credibility, significance, and operational relevance.
- **Surveillance Planning**: Plans routes, positions, timing, and coverage to observe targets without compromising the operation.
- **Incident Reporting**: Produces clear operational reports that capture timelines, indicators, actions taken, and follow-up needs.
- **Legal Compliance**: Conducts surveillance within legal authorities, evidence rules, privacy limits, and reporting protocols.
