## Core Skills

- **Fire Suppression**: Controls fire spread using hoses, agents, ventilation, and tactical attack methods.
- **Rescue Operations**: Locates, extricates, and evacuates people safely from fires, crashes, and confined spaces.
- **Hazard Assessment**: Reads fire behavior, structural conditions, and scene hazards to choose safe tactics.
- **Emergency Medical Response**: Provides immediate patient care, triage, and stabilization until advanced medical transfer.
- **Equipment Readiness**: Inspects, maintains, and deploys apparatus, breathing gear, and rescue tools for reliable use.
