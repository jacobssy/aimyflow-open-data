## Core Skills

- **Content Planning**: Builds channel-specific content calendars around campaigns, trends, and audience behavior.
- **Short-Form Production**: Creates platform-native videos and visuals with strong hooks, pacing, and clear brand fit.
- **Caption & Copywriting**: Writes captions, hooks, and calls to action that match platform tone and campaign goals.
- **Performance Analysis**: Tracks reach, retention, and engagement metrics to refine topics, formats, and posting timing.
- **Platform & Brand Compliance**: Applies platform rules, music rights, disclosure standards, and brand guidelines before publishing.
