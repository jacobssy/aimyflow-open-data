## Core Skills

- **Financial Modeling**: Builds integrated models to project performance, cash flow, and financing outcomes.
- **Valuation Analysis**: Assesses asset or company value using market multiples, DCF, and comparable transactions.
- **Budgeting & Forecasting**: Prepares budgets and rolling forecasts by linking operating drivers to financial results.
- **Variance Analysis**: Identifies and explains gaps between actuals, budget, and prior periods to support decisions.
- **Financial Reporting**: Produces clear management reports with KPIs, trends, and risk indicators for stakeholders.
