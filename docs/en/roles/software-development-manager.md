## Core Skills

- **Technical Roadmapping**: Turns product goals into phased engineering plans, dependencies, and delivery milestones.
- **Architecture Governance**: Guides design decisions to keep systems scalable, maintainable, and aligned across teams.
- **Delivery Management**: Manages scope, capacity, and execution risks to deliver reliable releases on schedule.
- **Engineering Operations**: Improves development flow through metrics, tooling, CI/CD practices, and incident feedback loops.
- **Security & Compliance Oversight**: Ensures delivery practices meet security, privacy, and regulatory requirements throughout the lifecycle.
