## Core Skills

- **Experimental Design**: Designs controlled studies that test physical hypotheses with measurable variables and valid comparisons.
- **Instrumentation & Measurement**: Operates, calibrates, and validates scientific instruments to produce precise and traceable data.
- **Data Analysis & Modeling**: Analyzes experimental results with statistical and physical models to quantify patterns, uncertainty, and fit.
- **Theory Validation**: Evaluates whether observations support, refine, or challenge existing physical theories and assumptions.
- **Laboratory Safety & Compliance**: Applies laboratory safety protocols, hazardous material controls, and documentation standards during research work.
