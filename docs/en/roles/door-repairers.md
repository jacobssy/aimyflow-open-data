## Core Skills

- **Fault Diagnosis**: Identifies the cause of sticking, sagging, impact damage, or hardware failure before repair begins.
- **Hinge and Frame Alignment**: Restores smooth door movement by adjusting hinges, frame position, clearances, and latch alignment.
- **Lock and Hardware Repair**: Repairs or replaces locks, closers, handles, rollers, and other door hardware to restore secure operation.
- **Surface and Panel Repair**: Repairs cracked panels, damaged edges, warped sections, and surface wear on wood, metal, or composite doors.
- **Fire and Safety Compliance**: Verifies that repaired doors still meet fire rating, egress, accessibility, and building hardware requirements.
