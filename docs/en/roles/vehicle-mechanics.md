## Core Skills

- **Fault Diagnosis**: Identifies mechanical and electronic faults using inspections, test equipment, and system data.
- **Engine and Drivetrain Repair**: Repairs and replaces engine, transmission, and drivetrain components to restore vehicle performance.
- **Brake and Suspension Service**: Inspects and services braking, steering, and suspension systems for safe road operation.
- **Electrical System Service**: Tests and repairs batteries, wiring, sensors, lighting, and charging or starting systems.
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Performs scheduled servicing, fluid changes, and wear checks to prevent breakdowns and extend vehicle life.
