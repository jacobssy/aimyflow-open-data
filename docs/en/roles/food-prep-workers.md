## Core Skills

- **Ingredient Preparation**: Wash, peel, cut, and portion ingredients to recipe and service requirements.
- **Food Safety Handling**: Handle, store, and label food correctly to meet hygiene and temperature standards.
- **Equipment Use**: Operate prep tools and kitchen equipment safely for consistent and efficient output.
- **Portion Control**: Measure and portion items accurately to support cost control and recipe consistency.
- **Prep Station Sanitation**: Clean and sanitize prep areas, surfaces, and tools throughout the shift.
