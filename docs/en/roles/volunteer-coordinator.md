## Core Skills

- **Volunteer Recruitment**: Sources, screens, and matches volunteers to roles based on program needs and availability.
- **Scheduling & Deployment**: Builds schedules, fills gaps, and confirms coverage for events, shifts, and ongoing services.
- **Onboarding & Training**: Delivers orientation, role instructions, and site procedures so volunteers can work safely and effectively.
- **Volunteer Compliance**: Maintains records, background checks, waivers, and policy adherence required for volunteer participation.
- **Retention & Recognition**: Tracks engagement, resolves volunteer concerns, and runs recognition efforts that support retention.
