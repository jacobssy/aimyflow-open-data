## Core Skills

- **Workforce Planning**: Forecasts staffing needs and aligns hiring plans with business demand and budget.
- **Talent Acquisition**: Oversees sourcing, selection, and offer processes to fill roles with qualified candidates.
- **Employee Relations**: Handles grievances, policy issues, and workplace conflicts with fair and consistent action.
- **Compensation & Benefits**: Designs pay structures and benefits programs that support retention, equity, and market competitiveness.
- **HR Compliance**: Ensures HR policies, records, and employment practices meet labor law and audit requirements.
