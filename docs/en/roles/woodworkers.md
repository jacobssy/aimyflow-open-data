## Core Skills

- **Material Selection**: Selects wood species and grades based on strength, grain, moisture, and end use.
- **Cutting & Shaping**: Cuts, planes, and shapes wood accurately to match dimensions, fit, and finish requirements.
- **Joinery Assembly**: Builds durable joints and assemblies using appropriate fasteners, adhesives, and joinery methods.
- **Surface Finishing**: Sands and applies stains, sealers, or coatings to protect wood and achieve the required appearance.
- **Shop Safety & Tool Care**: Operates saws and power tools safely while maintaining blades, bits, and equipment in working condition.
