## Core Skills

- **Client Intake Assessment**: Assesses housing history, risks, needs, and eligibility to determine immediate shelter support.
- **Case Planning**: Builds practical service plans with goals, referrals, and follow-up steps toward housing stability.
- **Crisis Intervention**: Responds to mental health, substance use, conflict, or safety incidents using shelter protocols.
- **Resource Navigation**: Connects residents with benefits, healthcare, legal aid, employment, and housing services.
- **Case Documentation**: Maintains accurate case notes, incident records, and compliance documentation for services and reporting.
