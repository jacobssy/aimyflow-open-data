## Core Skills

- **Script Development**: Shapes story structure, characters, and scenes into a producible independent film script.
- **Production Budgeting**: Builds realistic budgets and allocates limited funds across cast, crew, gear, and locations.
- **Directing on Set**: Leads performances, blocking, and shot execution to capture scenes efficiently under production constraints.
- **Post-Production Supervision**: Oversees editing, sound, music, and color to achieve a coherent final cut and delivery master.
- **Rights & Festival Delivery**: Secures clearances and prepares screeners, subtitles, and materials required for festivals and distribution.
