## Core Skills

- **Workplace Hazard Assessment**: Identifies physical, chemical, biological, and ergonomic hazards across work tasks and environments.
- **Exposure Monitoring**: Plans and interprets exposure measurements to verify control effectiveness and health risk levels.
- **Health Surveillance**: Coordinates medical surveillance programs for workers exposed to specific occupational health risks.
- **Occupational Health Compliance**: Applies occupational health regulations, standards, and documentation requirements in daily operations.
- **Control Measure Planning**: Designs practical control plans using elimination, engineering, administrative, and PPE measures.
