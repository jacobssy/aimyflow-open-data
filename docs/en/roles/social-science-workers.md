## Core Skills

- **Research Design**: Design studies with suitable methods, samples, and measures for valid social evidence.
- **Field Data Collection**: Gather survey, interview, observation, or archival data while maintaining consistency and quality.
- **Social Data Analysis**: Analyze qualitative and quantitative data to identify patterns, relationships, and social trends.
- **Ethical Research Compliance**: Protect participants through informed consent, privacy safeguards, and responsible data handling.
- **Policy Evaluation**: Assess program or policy effects and turn findings into practical recommendations.
