## Core Skills

- **Product Matching**: Matches customer needs to suitable products, features, and price points on the sales floor.
- **Sales Floor Merchandising**: Maintains displays, signage, and shelf presentation to support sales and brand standards.
- **POS & Cash Handling**: Processes transactions accurately, handles payments, and completes refunds or exchanges under store policy.
- **Inventory Control**: Monitors stock levels, replenishes shelves, and flags shortages or discrepancies promptly.
- **Store Policy Compliance**: Applies store procedures for pricing, age-restricted sales, loss prevention, and opening or closing tasks.
