## Core Skills

- **Care Needs Assessment**: Evaluates mobility, hygiene needs, skin condition, and daily living support requirements for each client.
- **Personal Care Planning**: Builds practical care plans that define routines, assistance levels, products, and safety precautions.
- **Hygiene & Skin Integrity**: Oversees bathing, grooming, continence care, and pressure area prevention to protect client health.
- **Care Team Scheduling**: Allocates staff and shift coverage according to client dependency levels, timing, and service priorities.
- **Care Documentation Compliance**: Maintains accurate care records, incident logs, consent details, and hygiene standards required by regulation.
