## Core Skills

- **Go-to-Market Planning**: Builds launch plans that align target segments, channels, pricing, and sales execution.
- **Pipeline Management**: Monitors funnel health, forecast accuracy, and deal progression to secure revenue targets.
- **Campaign Performance**: Optimizes campaigns using conversion, acquisition cost, and channel return data.
- **Pricing and Promotion**: Sets pricing and promotional tactics that balance competitiveness, margin, and sales volume.
- **CRM and Reporting**: Maintains CRM discipline and reporting standards to support segmentation, follow-up, and decisions.
