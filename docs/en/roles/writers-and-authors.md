## Core Skills

- **Narrative Development**: Builds plot, pacing, and character arcs into a coherent manuscript.
- **Research and Fact-Checking**: Validates sources, details, and context to keep content accurate and credible.
- **Voice and Style Control**: Maintains a consistent tone, diction, and stylistic approach for the intended readership.
- **Manuscript Revision**: Refines structure, language, and clarity through multiple editorial drafts.
- **Publishing Requirements**: Prepares work to meet submission guidelines, rights considerations, and editorial standards.
