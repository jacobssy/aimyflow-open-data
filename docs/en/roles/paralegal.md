## Core Skills

- **Legal Research**: Finds relevant statutes, cases, and regulations to support legal matters.
- **Document Drafting**: Prepares contracts, pleadings, and legal correspondence in correct format.
- **Case File Management**: Maintains organized case files, evidence, calendars, and document versions.
- **Court Filing Procedures**: Handles filing requirements, deadlines, and submission procedures for courts or agencies.
- **Due Diligence Review**: Reviews records and disclosures to identify legal risks and missing information.
