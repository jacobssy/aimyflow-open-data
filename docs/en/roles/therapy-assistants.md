## Core Skills

- **Therapy Session Support**: Assist therapists during sessions by preparing materials, positioning patients, and guiding prescribed exercises.
- **Patient Observation**: Monitor patient response, comfort, and functional changes during treatment and report relevant findings promptly.
- **Treatment Preparation**: Set up treatment areas, check equipment readiness, and maintain supplies needed for daily therapy workflows.
- **Progress Documentation**: Record session activities, patient participation, and basic progress notes accurately in the clinical record.
- **Safety and Infection Control**: Apply transfer safety, hygiene protocols, and infection control procedures to protect patients and staff.
