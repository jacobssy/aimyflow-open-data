## Core Skills

- **Course Planning**: Designs term syllabi, weekly lessons, and pacing aligned with program outcomes and student readiness.
- **Classroom Instruction**: Delivers lectures, discussions, labs, or demonstrations that make subject matter accessible to diverse learners.
- **Student Assessment**: Creates assignments and exams, applies grading criteria consistently, and gives actionable academic feedback.
- **Academic Advising**: Guides students on course expectations, study strategies, progression, and use of academic support resources.
- **Academic Records**: Maintains attendance, grades, learning records, and course documentation in line with institutional policies.
