## Core Skills

- **Laboratory Operations**: Prepares samples, sets up instruments, and runs routine laboratory procedures accurately.
- **Instrument Calibration**: Calibrates, verifies, and maintains scientific instruments to ensure reliable measurements.
- **Data Recording**: Records observations, measurements, and test conditions in complete and traceable form.
- **Quality Control Testing**: Performs standardized tests and checks results against specifications or reference values.
- **Lab Safety Compliance**: Handles chemicals, biological materials, and waste according to laboratory safety protocols.
