## Core Skills

- **Curriculum Design**: Designs course content, learning outcomes, and assessments that align with academic standards.
- **Scholarly Research**: Conducts original research, develops publishable findings, and advances knowledge in a discipline.
- **Student Assessment**: Evaluates student work with clear criteria and assigns grades that are fair and defensible.
- **Research Supervision**: Guides theses and research projects by shaping questions, methods, and academic rigor.
- **Academic Governance**: Handles committee work, policy implementation, and academic procedures required by the institution.
