## Core Skills

- **Risk Modeling**: Builds quantitative models to estimate exposure, probability, and potential loss under different scenarios.
- **Scenario Analysis**: Tests how market, credit, operational, or business shocks would affect portfolios and key metrics.
- **Risk Reporting**: Produces dashboards and reports that translate risk measurements into monitored limits, trends, and actions.
- **Control Assessment**: Reviews processes and controls to identify weaknesses, test effectiveness, and prioritize remediation needs.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Aligns risk methods, documentation, and monitoring with internal policy and external regulatory requirements.
