## Core Skills

- **Daily Living Assistance**: Supports eating, bathing, dressing, toileting, and mobility according to each person's condition.
- **Condition Monitoring**: Observes physical and behavioral changes and reports signs that may require prompt attention.
- **Care Plan Support**: Carries out assigned care tasks consistently in line with care plans, routines, and recorded needs.
- **Safety and Infection Control**: Applies safe handling, hygiene, and infection prevention procedures to protect care recipients and staff.
- **Care Documentation**: Records care provided, observed changes, and incidents accurately to support continuity and accountability.
