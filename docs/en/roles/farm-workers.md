## Core Skills

- **Crop Planting**: Prepare fields, sow or transplant crops, and maintain correct spacing and depth.
- **Irrigation Management**: Operate irrigation systems and adjust watering based on soil, weather, and crop stage.
- **Pest and Disease Monitoring**: Inspect crops for weeds, pests, and disease symptoms to support timely field treatment.
- **Harvest Handling**: Harvest at the right maturity and sort, pack, or store produce to protect quality.
- **Equipment Operation**: Use tractors, hand tools, and field equipment safely for daily planting, care, and harvest work.
