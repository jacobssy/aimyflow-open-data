## Core Skills

- **Machine Setup**: Set up presses, cutters, molders, or extruders to match material type, dimensions, and production tolerances.
- **Material Processing**: Cut, bend, mold, trim, or finish metal and plastic parts using the correct process for each material.
- **Dimensional Inspection**: Measure finished parts with gauges and instruments to verify dimensions, fit, and surface quality.
- **Production Monitoring**: Monitor runs, adjust feed rates or temperatures, and respond to defects to keep output stable.
- **Tool and Safety Control**: Inspect tooling, guards, and workstations to prevent damage, ensure safe operation, and reduce downtime.
