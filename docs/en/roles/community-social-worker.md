## Core Skills

- **Needs Assessment**: Assesses household, social, and welfare needs to determine appropriate community support.
- **Case Management**: Plans, coordinates, and reviews individualized support across services, timelines, and follow-up.
- **Resource Navigation**: Connects residents with benefits, housing, healthcare, legal aid, and local programs.
- **Safeguarding**: Identifies risk, responds to safeguarding concerns, and follows reporting and intervention protocols.
- **Community Outreach**: Builds local networks, identifies emerging issues, and organizes outreach and preventive activities.
