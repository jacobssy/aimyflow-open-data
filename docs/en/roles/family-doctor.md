## Core Skills

- **Primary Assessment**: Evaluates undifferentiated symptoms, medical history, and risk factors to form a safe first diagnosis.
- **Chronic Disease Management**: Monitors long-term conditions, adjusts treatment plans, and tracks control targets over time.
- **Preventive Care**: Plans screenings, vaccinations, and lifestyle interventions according to age, risk, and guidelines.
- **Referral Triage**: Recognizes when specialist referral, urgent workup, or hospital care is clinically necessary.
- **Medication Management**: Prescribes safely, reviews interactions and adherence, and updates medication lists across visits.
