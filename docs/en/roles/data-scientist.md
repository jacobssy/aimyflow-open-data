## Core Skills

- **Data Preparation**: Clean, join, and validate raw data so it is reliable for analysis and modeling.
- **Statistical Analysis**: Apply statistical methods to quantify patterns, uncertainty, and relationships in data.
- **Predictive Modeling**: Build and tune machine learning models that predict outcomes with measurable performance.
- **Model Evaluation**: Assess model quality with proper metrics, validation design, and error analysis before deployment.
- **Experiment Design**: Design A/B tests and observational studies that support credible product or business decisions.
