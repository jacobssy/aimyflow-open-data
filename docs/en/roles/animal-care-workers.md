## Core Skills

- **Animal Health Monitoring**: Observes behavior, appetite, and physical condition to detect illness or distress early.
- **Feeding and Nutrition**: Prepares and delivers species-appropriate diets according to schedules, portions, and care plans.
- **Habitat Sanitation**: Cleans enclosures, bedding, and equipment to maintain hygiene and reduce infection risk.
- **Safe Animal Handling**: Handles, restrains, and moves animals in ways that minimize stress and prevent injury.
- **Treatment Support**: Assists with medications, routine treatments, and care records under established instructions.
