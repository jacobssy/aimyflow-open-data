## Core Skills

- **Curriculum Alignment**: Aligns lesson content with vocational standards, certification targets, and employer expectations.
- **Workshop Instruction**: Demonstrates tools, techniques, and workflows while supervising hands-on practice safely.
- **Competency Assessment**: Assesses student performance against practical criteria and documents skill attainment reliably.
- **Training Safety Compliance**: Enforces workshop safety rules, equipment checks, and risk controls required for training environments.
- **Industry-Based Training Design**: Builds learning tasks and projects that reflect real workplace processes, tools, and quality standards.
