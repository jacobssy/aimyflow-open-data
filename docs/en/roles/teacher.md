## Core Skills

- **Lesson Planning**: Designs structured lessons with clear objectives, activities, and pacing for student learning.
- **Classroom Management**: Maintains an orderly, inclusive classroom environment that supports attention, behavior, and participation.
- **Student Assessment**: Evaluates student progress through assignments, observation, and tests to guide instruction.
- **Differentiated Instruction**: Adapts teaching methods and materials to different learning levels, needs, and abilities.
- **Curriculum Alignment**: Aligns teaching content and assessments with curriculum standards and school requirements.
