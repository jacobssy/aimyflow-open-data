## Core Skills

- **Threat Monitoring**: Monitor logs, alerts, and telemetry to detect suspicious activity across systems and networks.
- **Incident Response**: Investigate security events, contain affected assets, and coordinate recovery actions with evidence preserved.
- **Vulnerability Assessment**: Assess scan results and system exposure to prioritize vulnerabilities by exploitability and business impact.
- **Threat Intelligence Analysis**: Translate indicators, attacker techniques, and campaign reports into actionable detection and defense updates.
- **Security Control Review**: Evaluate the effectiveness of security controls and identify gaps against policy, risk, and compliance requirements.
