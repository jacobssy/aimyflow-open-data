## Core Skills

- **Material Handling**: Moves, stages, and protects construction materials so crews can work safely and without delays.
- **Site Preparation**: Clears work areas, sets up barriers, and prepares surfaces and tools before each task begins.
- **Tool and Equipment Support**: Selects, carries, and maintains basic tools and small equipment needed by skilled trades on site.
- **Debris Removal**: Collects, sorts, and removes debris to keep pathways clear and the site compliant and orderly.
- **Construction Safety Compliance**: Follows site safety rules, uses protective equipment correctly, and supports hazard control during daily tasks.
