## Core Skills

- **Site Preparation**: Prepares work areas by clearing debris, marking layouts, and setting up basic site protections.
- **Material Handling**: Moves, stores, and stages construction materials safely to support continuous site operations.
- **Tool and Equipment Operation**: Uses hand tools and site equipment correctly for cutting, fastening, mixing, and demolition tasks.
- **Structural Assembly**: Assists with assembling formwork, framing, and other building components according to site instructions.
- **Construction Safety Compliance**: Follows site safety rules, uses protective equipment, and identifies immediate hazards during daily work.
