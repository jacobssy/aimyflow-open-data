## Core Skills

- **Symptom Management**: Assesses pain, dyspnea, nausea, and agitation, then adjusts comfort measures and medications accordingly.
- **End-of-Life Care Planning**: Develops individualized care plans that align daily nursing actions with prognosis and patient goals.
- **Goals of Care Documentation**: Documents care preferences, advance directives, and code status accurately for safe clinical decision-making.
- **Terminal Medication Administration**: Administers subcutaneous, oral, and breakthrough medications safely while monitoring effect and adverse reactions.
- **Family Bereavement Support**: Supports families through decline and death by preparing them for changes and immediate post-death care.
