## Core Skills

- **Cataloging Standards**: Apply metadata and classification standards to make collections accurate, searchable, and interoperable.
- **Collection Development**: Select, acquire, and review materials based on user needs, scope, budget, and long-term relevance.
- **Archival Appraisal**: Determine which records have enduring value and set retention or transfer actions accordingly.
- **Preservation Management**: Protect physical and digital materials through handling rules, storage controls, and preservation workflows.
- **Access & Rights Control**: Provide appropriate access while managing copyright, privacy, restrictions, and records-use policies.
