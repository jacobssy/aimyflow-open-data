## Core Skills

- **Concept Development**: Translate briefs and audience needs into clear visual concepts and design directions.
- **Visual Composition**: Organize color, form, typography, and space to produce balanced and effective layouts.
- **Design Production**: Create polished artwork and production-ready files using relevant digital or physical techniques.
- **Material & Media Selection**: Choose materials, formats, and media based on aesthetics, function, cost, and context.
- **Quality & Brand Review**: Review work for visual consistency, technical accuracy, brand fit, and final output quality.
