## Core Skills

- **Legal Document Preparation**: Drafts, formats, and proofreads legal documents to meet court, client, and firm standards.
- **Court Filing Coordination**: Coordinates filings and service deadlines so submissions are complete, timely, and procedurally correct.
- **Case File Administration**: Maintains case files, correspondence, and supporting records in an accurate and accessible matter structure.
- **Legal Calendar Management**: Tracks hearings, limitation periods, and attorney deadlines to support reliable case progression.
- **Confidential Records Handling**: Handles sensitive legal and client information with proper access control, retention, and confidentiality practices.
