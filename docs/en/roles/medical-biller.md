## Core Skills

- **Claim Coding Review**: Checks diagnosis and procedure codes for accurate and billable claim submission.
- **Insurance Verification**: Confirms coverage, eligibility, and payer requirements before charges are billed.
- **Denial Management**: Investigates rejected claims, corrects errors, and resubmits for reimbursement.
- **Charge Entry**: Enters services, modifiers, and charges into billing systems with supporting detail.
- **Billing Compliance**: Applies payer rules and healthcare billing regulations to reduce audit risk.
