## Core Skills

- **Electrical Fault Diagnosis**: Diagnoses faults in motors, controls, wiring, and sensors using meters, schematics, and test procedures.
- **Mechanical Assembly Repair**: Repairs and aligns gearboxes, bearings, couplings, and drive components to restore reliable machine operation.
- **Control Circuit Wiring**: Installs and modifies control panels and machine wiring according to diagrams, terminal plans, and standards.
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Performs inspections, lubrication, part replacement, and functional checks to prevent equipment breakdowns.
- **Electrical Safety Compliance**: Applies isolation, lockout, grounding, and verification procedures to service equipment safely and compliantly.
