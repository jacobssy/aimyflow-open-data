## Core Skills

- **Quantitative Modeling**: Builds mathematical models to represent systems, test assumptions, and generate usable predictions.
- **Experimental Design**: Plans experiments with controlled variables, sound methodology, and measurable outcomes.
- **Data Analysis**: Processes and interprets scientific or numerical data to identify patterns, uncertainty, and significance.
- **Technical Computation**: Uses computational tools to solve equations, simulate processes, and automate analytical workflows.
- **Scientific Reporting**: Documents methods, results, and limitations in a reproducible format suitable for review or application.
