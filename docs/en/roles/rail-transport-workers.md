## Core Skills

- **Train Operations Control**: Manage train movements safely and on time by following dispatch orders, signals, and operating rules.
- **Shunting and Yard Handling**: Couple, uncouple, position, and assemble rail vehicles correctly for departures, arrivals, and yard movements.
- **Brake and Safety Inspection**: Inspect brakes, couplers, doors, and visible defects before movement to confirm the train is fit to run.
- **Passenger and Freight Handling**: Handle boarding, loading, securing, and platform or freight procedures in line with service and safety requirements.
- **Railway Safety Compliance**: Apply railway regulations, emergency procedures, and site protections consistently during normal and disrupted operations.
