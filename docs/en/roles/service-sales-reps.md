## Core Skills

- **Needs Assessment**: Identifies client service gaps, usage patterns, and business priorities to target the right offer.
- **Service Proposal Design**: Builds tailored service packages, scopes, and value cases that fit customer operations and budgets.
- **Contract Negotiation**: Negotiates pricing, service levels, terms, and renewal conditions while protecting margin and feasibility.
- **Pipeline Forecasting**: Tracks opportunities, renewal timing, and conversion risk to maintain an accurate service sales forecast.
- **Account Expansion**: Grows recurring revenue by cross-selling service upgrades, renewals, and additional coverage within existing accounts.
