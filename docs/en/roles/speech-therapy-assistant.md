## Core Skills

- **Therapy Session Support**: Prepares materials and guides practice tasks during speech therapy sessions under clinician direction.
- **Progress Observation**: Tracks patient responses, participation, and speech performance to support ongoing treatment adjustments.
- **Home Practice Guidance**: Explains assigned home exercises to patients or caregivers so practice stays accurate between sessions.
- **Clinical Documentation**: Maintains accurate session notes, attendance records, and treatment data in line with clinical procedures.
- **Safety and Equipment Setup**: Sets up therapy spaces and speech tools safely, cleanly, and ready for each patient’s needs.
