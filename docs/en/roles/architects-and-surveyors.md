## Core Skills

- **Site Surveying**: Measures land, levels, and existing conditions to establish accurate project baselines.
- **Architectural Drafting**: Produces coordinated drawings, plans, and sections that can be built and reviewed.
- **Boundary & Cadastre**: Determines legal boundaries and cadastral data for permits, transfers, and site control.
- **Code Compliance**: Checks designs against zoning, building codes, setbacks, access, and safety requirements.
- **Set-Out Control**: Transfers design coordinates to the site so structures are positioned and built correctly.
