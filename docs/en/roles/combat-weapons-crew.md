## Core Skills

- **Weapons Operation**: Operates assigned weapon systems safely and effectively under combat conditions.
- **Target Acquisition**: Identifies, tracks, and confirms targets using sighting, sensor, and range data.
- **Fire Control**: Applies firing data, engagement rules, and timing to deliver accurate effects.
- **Ammunition Handling**: Selects, loads, stores, and accounts for ammunition in line with safety procedures.
- **Weapons Maintenance**: Inspects, services, and troubleshoots weapon systems to keep them mission ready.
