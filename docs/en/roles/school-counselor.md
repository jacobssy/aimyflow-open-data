## Core Skills

- **Student Assessment**: Assesses students’ academic, behavioral, and emotional needs to guide appropriate support.
- **Counseling Intervention**: Provides structured individual or group counseling for school-related and personal concerns.
- **Academic Planning**: Builds course, graduation, and postsecondary plans aligned with student goals and requirements.
- **Crisis Response**: Responds to safety concerns, mental health crises, and mandated reporting situations in school.
- **Support Coordination**: Coordinates with teachers, families, and external services to implement student support plans.
