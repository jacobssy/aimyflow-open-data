## Core Skills

- **Cardiac Diagnosis**: Interprets symptoms, ECGs, imaging, and lab results to identify cardiovascular disease accurately.
- **Echocardiography**: Performs and reads echocardiograms to assess cardiac structure, function, and valve disease.
- **Acute Cardiac Care**: Stabilizes and treats urgent conditions such as myocardial infarction, arrhythmia, and heart failure.
- **Cardiovascular Treatment Planning**: Builds evidence-based treatment plans using medication, monitoring, rehabilitation, and procedural referral.
- **Hemodynamic Assessment**: Assesses circulation and cardiac performance to guide management in complex cardiovascular cases.
