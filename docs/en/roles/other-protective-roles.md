## Core Skills

- **Threat Assessment**: Identifies suspicious behavior, environmental risks, and escalation indicators to guide protective action.
- **Access Control**: Verifies identity, authorization, and restricted-area entry in line with site security rules.
- **Patrol Operations**: Conducts structured patrols to detect hazards, security breaches, and unsafe conditions across assigned areas.
- **Incident Response**: Applies security procedures during disturbances, emergencies, or breaches to contain risk and protect people.
- **Security Reporting**: Produces clear logs and incident reports that support compliance, evidence handling, and follow-up action.
