## Core Skills

- **Patient Intake**: Collects essential patient details, confirms referrals, and prepares records for the care team.
- **Clinical Documentation**: Maintains accurate charts, updates service notes, and files forms according to care procedures.
- **Appointment Coordination**: Schedules visits, arranges follow-up services, and manages timing changes across patients and staff.
- **Infection Control Support**: Applies hygiene routines, handles materials safely, and supports infection prevention requirements in daily operations.
- **Equipment Preparation**: Prepares rooms, checks basic equipment readiness, and restocks supplies needed for routine care tasks.
