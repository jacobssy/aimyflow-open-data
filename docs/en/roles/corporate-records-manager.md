## Core Skills

- **Records Retention Scheduling**: Defines retention and disposition rules for corporate records based on legal, operational, and historical requirements.
- **Document Classification**: Builds file plans, metadata structures, and indexing standards so records can be retrieved and governed consistently.
- **Records Compliance Control**: Monitors recordkeeping practices against regulatory, privacy, audit, and litigation hold requirements.
- **Digital Records Governance**: Sets controls for electronic records in business systems, including access, versioning, capture, and preservation.
- **Disposition and Archival Transfer**: Executes defensible destruction, archival transfer, and chain-of-custody processes for records at end of life.
