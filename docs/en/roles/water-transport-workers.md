## Core Skills

- **Vessel Handling**: Control vessel movement safely during docking, departure, and confined-water operations.
- **Cargo Securing**: Stow and secure cargo to maintain stability and prevent shifting in transit.
- **Navigation Watch**: Maintain lookout, monitor position, and follow route and traffic instructions during voyages.
- **Marine Safety Compliance**: Apply maritime safety procedures, emergency drills, and regulatory checks in daily operations.
- **Deck Equipment Operation**: Operate winches, lines, ramps, and deck gear for mooring and loading tasks.
